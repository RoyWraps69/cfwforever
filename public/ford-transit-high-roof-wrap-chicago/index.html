After the Wrap — Care, Warranty, and Reorders

Every Ford Transit High Roof wrap comes with our comprehensive 2-year workmanship warranty, which covers issues like lifting, bubbling, edge peeling, and any installation defects. This is distinct from the manufacturer's vinyl warranty; both Avery Dennison and 3M offer their own film performance warranties, typically ranging from 5–7 years for outdoor usage.

To maximize the lifespan of your Ford Transit High Roof wrap, hand wash it using mild soap and water, and always steer clear of automated brush car washes, which can scratch the vinyl and lift edges. When using pressure washers, keep them below 1,200 PSI and at least 12 inches away from panel edges. Promptly remove bird droppings and tree sap, as their acidic compounds can etch into the vinyl if left too long.

Should you expand your fleet by adding more Ford Transit High Roof vans in the future, your brand's design files are securely stored with us indefinitely. We never discard fleet artwork. This ensures that reorders for your new Transit High Roof vans go into production much faster, as the initial design approval is already complete.
