Cast vs. Calendered — the decision most shops hide from you

Two types of vinyl are used in vehicle wraps. Most shops don't tell you which one they're installing. It's the single biggest factor in how long your wrap lasts.

CAST VINYL — WHAT CFW USES
5–7 Years

Avery Dennison MPI 1105 or 3M IJ180-CV3. Conforms to complex curves, survives Chicago winters and road salt, holds color. Every CFW install uses cast vinyl. No exceptions.

CALENDERED VINYL — DISCOUNT SHOPS
12–18 Mo.

Cheaper upfront. Shrinks in cold weather, lifts at edges, fades faster. Common at discount shops. Almost always costs more in the long run when you factor in early replacement.

How to make your wrap last longer

Hand wash only
Automated brush car washes cause edge lifting. Use a soft cloth and wrap-safe cleaner. This alone adds 6-12 months to wrap life.
Stay below 1,200 PSI
If using a pressure washer, keep below 1,200 PSI at 40 degrees. High pressure lifts edges, especially around door handles.
Garage when possible
UV and temperature cycling degrade vinyl fastest. Indoor storage adds 1-2 years of lifespan to any wrap.
Rinse road salt immediately
Salt is the #1 wrap killer in Chicago. Rinse rocker panels and lower sections after heavy salt days between December and March.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a vehicle wrap last in Chicago?

Premium cast vinyl wraps (Avery Dennison MPI 1105 or 3M IJ180-CV3) last 5-7 years in Chicago's climate. With proper care, Chicago Fleet Wraps clients regularly see 6-7 years despite road salt and harsh winters.

What makes a vehicle wrap last longer?

Three factors: vinyl grade (cast vs. calendered), installation quality, and maintenance. Hand washing instead of automated brush car washes is the single biggest maintenance factor.

How long do fleet wraps last on work vans?

Based on 9,400+ Chicago installs: cargo vans average 5.2 years, box trucks 6.4 years, pickup trucks 4.8 years using 3M IJ180-CV3 with 8519 laminate. Most common failure point is lower rocker panels from road salt.

Does Chicago weather shorten vehicle wrap lifespan?

Chicago is one of the harshest climates for wraps — road salt, sub-zero temps, 95F summers, intense UV. Premium cast vinyl handles this. Cheap calendered vinyl fails in 12-18 months. Chicago Fleet Wraps uses cast vinyl exclusively.

When should I replace a vehicle wrap?

Replace when you see significant fading, large peeling areas, cracking, or when business info changes. Minor edge lifting can be spot-repaired. Chicago Fleet Wraps keeps production files on record for every client for fast, color-matched replacements.
