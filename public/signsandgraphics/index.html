Why Vehicle Graphics?

Vehicle graphics are the most cost-effective form of local advertising available. A single branded van generates 30,000–70,000 impressions per day in Chicago traffic. Unlike digital ads or billboards, there's no recurring cost — one investment that works for 5–7 years.

For small businesses, vehicle graphics are often the first step into fleet branding. Simple lettering and logo decals can be applied in under a day, delivering immediate brand visibility on every job, every commute, and every errand.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of vehicle graphics do you offer?

Die-cut decals, printed graphics, lettering, DOT numbers, partial wraps, window graphics, fleet packages, and yard signs.

How much do vehicle graphics cost?

Die-cut decals start at $150. Lettering packages start at $250. Partial wraps start at $800. Fleet pricing with volume discounts.

How long do vehicle graphics last?

Cut vinyl: 7–10 years. Printed graphics with UV overlaminate: 5–7 years. All using premium Avery Dennison or 3M materials.

Do you design custom graphics?

Yes. In-house designers create graphics on exact vehicle templates. You approve a digital proof before production.

Can you match graphics across my fleet?

Yes. Fleet graphics programs ensure consistent branding. Templates stored for future additions.
