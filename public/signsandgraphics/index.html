Why Vehicle Graphics?

Vehicle graphics are the most cost-effective form of local advertising available. A single branded van generates 30,000–70,000 impressions per day in Chicago traffic. Unlike digital ads or billboards, there's no recurring cost — one investment that works for 5–7 years.

For small businesses, vehicle graphics are often the first step into fleet branding. Simple lettering and logo decals can be applied in under a day, delivering immediate brand visibility on every job, every commute, and every errand.
