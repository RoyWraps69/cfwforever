Signs & Vehicle Graphics

Custom signs and vehicle graphics for businesses across Chicago. From simple die-cut lettering to complex multi-color fleet graphics, we design and install graphics that get your brand noticed on the road.

Our Graphics Services

  • Die-cut vinyl decals — logos, phone numbers, and website URLs
  • Vehicle lettering — DOT numbers, fleet identification, regulatory text
  • Partial wraps — accent panels, tailgates, and hood graphics
  • Window graphics — perforated vinyl for rear windows and storefronts
  • Fleet graphics packages — consistent branding across multiple vehicles
  • Yard signs and banners — job site and event signage

Why Vehicle Graphics?

Vehicle graphics are the most cost-effective form of local advertising. A single branded van generates 30,000–70,000 impressions per day in Chicago traffic. Unlike digital ads, there's no recurring cost — one investment that works for 5–7 years.

Materials

We use only premium cast vinyl from Avery Dennison and 3M. Cast vinyl conforms to curves and rivets without lifting, and is rated for 5–7 years outdoor durability in Chicago weather.
