What Determines Fleet Program Wrap Cost

Fleet wrap pricing is based on the same per-vehicle rates as individual wraps, with volume discounts applied to the total invoice. The more vehicles, the higher the discount. Fleet programs also benefit from design efficiency — once the brand standard is established and approved on vehicle #1, subsequent vehicles are faster and less expensive to design. For fleets with multiple vehicle types, we adapt the approved design to each vehicle template at no additional cost.

What is Included in Every Fleet Wrap

Fleet programs include everything in standard individual wraps plus: dedicated fleet account management, permanent file storage for your brand assets, priority scheduling for new vehicle additions, phased installation scheduling to minimize operational impact, consistent Pantone color matching across all print runs, and a single point of contact for all fleet-related needs. Fleet accounts do not expire — when you add vehicles 3 years from now, your files are ready.
