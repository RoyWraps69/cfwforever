Fleet Wrap Rollout Process

Our fleet rollout process is designed to minimize vehicle downtime while ensuring brand consistency across your entire fleet:

  • 1. Fleet Assessment — We inventory your vehicles by make, model, and year to create accurate templates.
  • 2. Design & Brand Standards — We create a master design template that works across all vehicle types.
  • 3. Client Approval — Full-color mockups on every vehicle type before production begins.
  • 4. Phased Installation — We wrap 1–3 vehicles at a time so your fleet stays operational.
  • 5. Quality Control — Multi-point inspection on every vehicle before release.
  • 6. Ongoing Support — Replacement wraps for new vehicles and damage repairs.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do fleet wraps cost in Chicago?

Pricing depends on vehicle type: cargo vans $2,800–$4,500, sprinters $3,500–$5,200, box trucks $4,200–$10,000, pickups $2,200–$3,800. Volume discounts of 5–15% apply for fleets of 3+ vehicles.

Who needs fleet wraps?

Any business with 3+ vehicles: HVAC, plumbing, electrical, contracting, delivery, moving, landscaping, and any service business with vehicles on the road daily.

How does the fleet rollout process work?

We create a master design template, then wrap 1–3 vehicles at a time in phases so your fleet stays operational. A 10-vehicle fleet typically completes in 4–6 weeks.

Are fleet wraps tax deductible?

Yes — fleet wraps are 100% tax deductible as a business advertising expense under IRS Section 179. The full cost can be deducted in the year of installation.
