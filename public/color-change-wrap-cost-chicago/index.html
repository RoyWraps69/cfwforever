What Determines Color Change Wrap Wrap Cost

Color change wrap cost is driven by vehicle size, panel complexity, and film choice. Standard gloss and matte films use Avery Dennison Supreme Wrapping Film or 3M 2080 series at $8.25/sqft. Specialty films — chrome, color shift, holographic, brushed metal — are priced at $11–$18/sqft due to the premium material cost. Sports cars with compound curves, door sills, and complex body sculpting take more installation time than standard vehicles and are priced accordingly.
