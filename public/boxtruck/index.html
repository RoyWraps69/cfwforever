Understanding Box Truck Wrap Pricing in Chicagoland

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to wrap a 20-foot box truck at your Chicago facility?

For a typical 20-foot box truck, a full wrap installation at our N Lawndale Ave location usually takes between 3 to 4 business days, depending on the design complexity and vehicle condition. We aim for efficient turnaround without compromising our meticulous quality standards.

What's the cost difference between a full wrap and a partial wrap for a 24-foot box truck?

For a 24-foot box truck, a full wrap can range from $8,000 to $11,000+. A partial wrap, focusing on high-impact areas like the rear doors and sides, typically costs between $3,500 and $6,000, offering a more budget-friendly option with significant visual impact.

Can you wrap a box truck with a corrugated or riveted surface without issues?

Absolutely. Our expert installers are highly skilled in wrapping box trucks with corrugated sides and numerous rivets. We exclusively use premium cast vinyls like Avery Dennison MPI 1105 and 3M IJ180-CV3, which are specifically designed to conform perfectly to these challenging surfaces, ensuring a seamless and durable finish.

Do you offer design services for box truck wraps, or do I need to provide my own artwork?

Yes, we offer comprehensive in-house design services tailored for box truck dimensions. Our graphic designers can work with your existing branding guidelines or create a completely new, impactful design from scratch. We'll provide proofs for your approval before production begins.

What kind of warranty do you provide for box truck wraps installed in Chicago?

Chicago Fleet Wraps offers a robust 2-year workmanship warranty on all our box truck wraps. This covers any issues related to installation, giving you peace of mind that your investment is protected against bubbling, lifting, or peeling under normal conditions.

How does a box truck wrap compare to painting for branding my delivery fleet in Chicago?

A box truck wrap is significantly more versatile and cost-effective than painting for branding. Wraps offer vibrant, high-resolution graphics, protect the original paint, and can be removed cleanly, preserving resale value. Painting is permanent, more expensive for complex designs, and offers limited design flexibility for changing promotions or branding updates.

Can you handle wrapping an entire fleet of box trucks for a large Chicago-based company?

Yes, Chicago Fleet Wraps is fully equipped to handle large-scale fleet wrapping projects. We have the capacity, materials, and skilled installers to manage multiple box trucks efficiently, minimizing downtime for your operations. We also offer free fleet pickup and delivery across Chicagoland to streamline the process.

What specific industries in Chicago benefit most from box truck wraps?

Many industries in Chicago benefit immensely, including HVAC, plumbing, electrical, landscaping, construction, and delivery services for food or packages. Any business with box trucks operating daily across neighborhoods like Andersonville, Cicero, or Oak Park can gain significant brand exposure and generate leads with a professional wrap.
