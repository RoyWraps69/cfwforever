Expert Insight

What to Look for in a Vehicle Wrap Shop Near You

Not all wrap shops are equal. Before you commit to any wrap installer, ask these questions:

What vinyl brands do they use?

The two industry standards are Avery Dennison and 3M. If a shop uses Oracal, Hexis, or unnamed Chinese vinyl, the wrap will fail faster, especially in Chicago's freeze-thaw climate. Cast vinyl conforms to curves and rivets without lifting. Calendered vinyl shrinks, cracks, and peels within 18 months on a vehicle that sees daily commercial use.

Do they have a dedicated wrap bay?

Vinyl application requires a clean, temperature-controlled environment. Dust particles trapped under the vinyl create bubbles that expand in summer heat. A shop wrapping vehicles outdoors or in an open garage is cutting corners that will cost you in 6 months.

How many vehicles have they wrapped?

Experience matters. A shop that has wrapped 50 vehicles is still learning. A shop that has wrapped 9,400+ vehicles has seen every panel configuration, every rivet pattern, every curve that causes inexperienced installers to fail. Chicago Fleet Wraps has been installing commercial wraps for 24 years — longer than most wrap shops have existed.

Do they offer fleet pickup and delivery?

If you're wrapping a fleet of 5-50 vehicles, you can't afford to have drivers shuttling vehicles back and forth. We pick up your vehicles from your lot, wrap them in our facility, and deliver them back — free of charge across all of Chicagoland. No other wrap shop near Chicago offers this for fleets of any size.

What's their warranty?

A confident shop warranties their work. We provide a 2-year workmanship warranty on every installation. If any edge lifts, any seam separates, or any panel fails due to installation error — we fix it at no charge. The Avery Dennison material warranty covers the vinyl itself for 5-7 years against fading, cracking, and delamination.
