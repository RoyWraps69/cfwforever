Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a commercial fleet wrap last?

Avery Dennison MPI 1105 and 3M IJ180-CV3 are rated 5–7 years outdoor. With proper care, commercial fleet wraps in Chicago regularly reach year 6–7 looking sharp.

How does fleet pricing work?

3–4 vehicles: 5% off. 5–9 vehicles: 10% off. 10+ vehicles: 15% off. Pricing is per vehicle and fleet discounts apply automatically.

Do you handle design?

Yes. The team handles design in-house on exact vehicle templates. You approve before we print. Most clients have no existing wrap artwork — we build it from scratch.

What happens if a panel gets damaged?

Cast vinyl can be spot-repaired. Matching panels can be recut and replaced without redoing the entire wrap.
