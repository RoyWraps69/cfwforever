Comprehensive Care for Your Wrapped Fleet

Maintaining the pristine appearance and longevity of your commercial vehicle wraps is straightforward with a few simple practices. We recommend hand-washing your wrapped vehicles with mild, non-abrasive detergents and a soft cloth, avoiding high-pressure washes that can lift edges, especially around intricate graphics. Regular cleaning prevents grime buildup from Chicago's urban environment.

For minor scuffs or dirt, a quick wipe-down with an isopropyl alcohol solution (70% alcohol, 30% water) can often do the trick. Avoid waxing the wrap, as some waxes can degrade the vinyl. With proper care, your investment will continue to shine, projecting a professional image for your Chicago business as it travels from Evanston to Joliet and beyond.
