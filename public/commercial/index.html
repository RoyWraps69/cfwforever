Materials — Cast Vinyl Only

We exclusively use cast vinyl — never calendered. Cast vinyl conforms to curves, rivets, and corrugations without lifting. It stays flat through Chicago winters (-20°F to 100°F temperature swings) and doesn't shrink, crack, or yellow.

  • Avery Dennison MPI 1105 — SuperCast film with EZ Apply RS adhesive. 2.5 mil. Air-release channels. 7-year outdoor rating.
  • 3M IJ180-CV3 — Controltac with Comply adhesive. 2 mil. Repositionable during install. 7-year outdoor rating.
  • UV Overlaminate — Avery DOL 1000Z (gloss) or DOL 1300Z (matte). Standard on every install.
  • HP Latex Printing — Water-based latex inks. Pantone-matched. Consistent color across fleet vehicles printed weeks apart.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a commercial fleet wrap last?

Avery Dennison MPI 1105 and 3M IJ180-CV3 are rated 5–7 years outdoor. With proper care — hand washing and avoiding automated car washes with brushes — commercial fleet wraps in Chicago regularly reach year 6–7 looking sharp.

How does fleet pricing work?

Pricing is per vehicle based on size and coverage. Fleet discounts apply automatically: 3% for 2–4 vehicles, 7% for 5–9, 11% for 10–24, 15% for 25+. Identical designs across multiple vehicles cost less per unit because templates are reused.

Do you handle design or do I need to bring artwork?

We handle everything in-house. Our designers build your wrap on exact vehicle templates — not generic mockups. You approve a digital proof before we print. Most clients have no existing wrap artwork — we build it from your logo and brand guidelines.

What happens if a panel gets damaged?

Cast vinyl can be spot-repaired. We keep your design files on record so matching panels can be recut and replaced without redoing the entire wrap. Typical panel replacement costs $200–$600.

Can you wrap leased fleet vehicles?

Yes. Cast vinyl wraps are fully removable without paint damage. In fact, wraps protect the factory paint from UV damage, road salt, and minor scratches — often improving lease return condition.

How many impressions does a wrapped fleet vehicle generate?

A single wrapped vehicle generates 30,000–70,000 daily impressions in Chicago metro traffic, according to the Outdoor Advertising Association of America. A fleet of 10 vehicles can deliver 300,000–700,000 impressions per day at a CPM under $0.50.

Is a commercial vehicle wrap tax deductible?

Yes. Vehicle wraps are 100% tax deductible as a business advertising expense. Consult your accountant, but most commercial wraps qualify under standard advertising deductions or IRS Section 179.

Do you offer free pickup and delivery?

Yes. Free pickup and delivery for all fleet vehicles throughout Chicagoland — Cook, DuPage, Lake, Will, and Kane counties. We schedule pickups to minimize disruption to your operations.
