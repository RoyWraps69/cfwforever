Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free pickup in Lombard?

Yes — we provide free fleet pickup and delivery throughout Lombard and all of DuPage County. Our Chicago facility is approximately 25 minutes from downtown Lombard via I-290 or I-88.

How much do fleet wraps cost for Lombard businesses?

Cargo van wraps start at $3,150, sprinter vans at $3,700, and box trucks at $4,500. Fleet discounts of 5–15% apply for businesses wrapping 3 or more vehicles. All fleet wraps are 100% tax deductible under IRS Section 179.

How long do fleet wraps last?

Our wraps use premium cast vinyl rated for 5–7 years of outdoor durability. Combined with our 2-year installation warranty covering lifting, peeling, and bubbling, your investment is fully protected for years of commercial use.

Can I wrap leased vehicles?

Absolutely. Vinyl wraps are non-permanent and can be professionally removed without any damage to the original paint. Many Lombard businesses wrap leased cargo vans and return them in pristine condition at lease end.

How long does a fleet wrap last on a Lombard delivery vehicle?

Using Avery Dennison MPI 1105 or 3M IJ180-CV3 cast vinyl, fleet wraps last 5–7 years under normal conditions. For high-mileage Lombard delivery vehicles, we recommend a UV-protective overlaminate that extends the wrap's appearance and protects against Illinois sun exposure.

Can you wrap our Lombard fleet vehicles at our location?

We offer free pickup and delivery for all Lombard fleet wrap projects — we come to your Lombard facility, pick up the vehicles, wrap them at our Chicago shop, and return them. For large fleets, we can also arrange on-site installation at your Lombard location.
