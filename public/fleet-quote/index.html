Frequently Asked

Fleet quote questions

Who handles my fleet account once I submit?

You're assigned a single dedicated point of contact within 2 hours — one person who walks you from quote through design, install, and post-rollout support. No call-center handoffs, no losing context, no being routed through three departments. Your contact stays the same for the life of the program.

How fast do you respond to a fleet quote?

Real per-vehicle pricing in your inbox within 2 business hours during 8 am – 5 pm CT, Monday through Friday. Requests submitted overnight get a response by 10 am the next business day.

What should I include in my fleet manifest?

Year/make/model and quantity of each vehicle is enough to start. PDF, CSV, or XLSX uploads work. If you have brand assets (logo, colors, existing artwork) attach those too — it saves design time. If you don't have artwork, our in-house design team handles it at no extra cost.

How do you stage rollouts so my fleet stays running?

Fleet rollouts are scheduled at 6-10 vehicles per week so the rest of your fleet stays on the road. We pick up at your facility, wrap at our 4711 N. Lamon Ave shop, and return same-week. A 50-vehicle program typically rolls out across 5-7 weeks.

What does a fleet walkthrough call cover?

30-minute video or phone call where your assigned point of contact reviews your fleet manifest, confirms pricing tier, walks through design timeline, schedules pickup windows, and answers operational questions. No sales pitch, no obligation.
