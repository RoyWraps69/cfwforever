After the Wrap — Care, Warranty, and Reorders

Every Nissan NV wrap installation comes with a robust 2-year workmanship warranty, safeguarding against issues like lifting, bubbling, edge peeling, and any installation defects. This is distinct from the manufacturer's vinyl warranty; both Avery Dennison and 3M provide their own film performance warranties, typically ranging from 5 to 7 years for outdoor usage on your Nissan NV.

To maximize the lifespan of your Nissan NV wrap: always hand wash with mild soap and water, steer clear of automated brush car washes (as they can scratch and lift edges), maintain pressure washer settings below 1,200 PSI and at least 12 inches from panel edges, and promptly address bird droppings and tree sap – these acidic substances can etch into the vinyl if left for extended periods.

When you expand your fleet with additional Nissan NV vans in the future, your unique brand files are permanently stored within our system. We never discard fleet artwork. This ensures that reorders for new Nissan NV wraps move into production much faster, as the design approval process is already complete – we simply adapt your existing brand standards to the new Nissan NV cargo van template and submit for final confirmation.
