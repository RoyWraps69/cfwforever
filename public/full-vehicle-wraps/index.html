What Does a Full Vehicle Wrap Include?

A full vehicle wrap covers both sides, rear, hood, roof, bumpers, mirrors, and door handles — plus door jambs visible when doors are open. A sedan uses 220–260 sq ft of vinyl, an SUV uses 270–320 sq ft, and a cargo van uses 280–350 sq ft. Pricing ranges from $2,800 to $5,500.

Care and Maintenance for Your Full Vehicle Wrap

Maintaining the pristine appearance and longevity of your full vehicle wrap is straightforward and essential for maximizing its impact. We recommend regular hand washing with mild automotive soap and water, avoiding abrasive brushes or high-pressure washers that can damage the vinyl. For stubborn grime or bird droppings, prompt cleaning is best to prevent staining or etching of the film. We also advise against automatic car washes, especially those with rotating brushes, which can scratch the wrap's surface.

Parking your fully wrapped vehicle in shaded areas when possible, particularly during peak summer months, can help protect the vinyl from prolonged UV exposure and extend its vibrant color life. With proper care, your full vehicle wrap, made from Avery Dennison MPI 1105 or 3M IJ180-CV3, will continue to look exceptional and promote your brand effectively across Chicago for 5-7 years or even longer. Our team provides detailed care instructions upon completion of every full wrap installation.

Full Wrap Coverage — What's Actually Included

Standard full wrap coverage: Both sides, rear, hood, roof, bumpers, and door jambs visible when doors are open. Mirrors and door handles are wrapped. Window trim is typically excluded unless specifically requested. Roof coverage depends on vehicle height — we wrap roofs on sedans and SUVs but not on high-roof vans (not visible from ground level).

Material usage: A full sedan wrap uses 220–260 sq ft of vinyl. A full SUV uses 270–320 sq ft. A full cargo van uses 280–350 sq ft. We print 15% overage to account for trimming waste and any panels that need reprinting during installation.

Our full wrap includes: Custom design with 3D vehicle template mockup, printing on 3M IJ180Cv3, 8519 gloss or matte lamination, surface decontamination (clay bar + IPA wipe), professional installation, post-heat of all edges and recesses, and a 2-year labor warranty against peeling, bubbling, or lifting.
