Professional Installation Process

Our installation process follows industry-standard protocols to ensure durability and visual perfection:

1. Vehicle Assessment & Preparation

We begin with a thorough inspection of your Rivian's surface, identifying any existing damage or imperfections. The vehicle is then cleaned using pH-neutral soap and clay bar treatment to remove all contaminants. This critical step ensures perfect vinyl adhesion.

2. Design Finalization

Your custom design is optimized for the R1T, R1S, or R2's specific geometry. We use precision measurement and digital mockups to preview the final result. Any adjustments are made before installation begins.

3. Vinyl Application

Premium cast vinyl is applied in sections, with technicians using heat guns and squeegees to achieve bubble-free, perfectly aligned coverage. The process accounts for Rivian's curved surfaces, panel gaps, and integrated design elements.

4. Edge Sealing & Quality Control

All edges are sealed with protective coating to prevent moisture intrusion and peeling. Final inspection includes high-resolution photography and a walk-through with the client.

Maintenance & Care

Proper maintenance extends your wrap's lifespan significantly. Avoid pressure washing for the first 72 hours post-installation. Thereafter, hand wash monthly with mild soap and soft microfiber cloths. Avoid automatic car washes and abrasive sponges. Apply UV protectant every 6 months to maintain color vibrancy.
