Pickup Truck Wraps Chicago

Professional pickup truck wraps for contractors, service companies, and businesses across Chicago. Whether you need a full wrap, partial wrap, or simple lettering — we've wrapped thousands of pickup trucks over 24+ years.

Trucks We Wrap

  • Ford F-150, F-250, F-350 — America's best-selling truck, templated for exact fit
  • Chevrolet Silverado 1500/2500/3500 — full and partial wrap options
  • RAM 1500, 2500, 3500 — including crew cab and extended bed
  • Toyota Tundra and Tacoma — mid-size and full-size templates
  • GMC Sierra — all cab configurations
  • Nissan Titan and Frontier — work truck wrap packages

Wrap Options

Full wraps cover every painted surface for maximum impact — cab, bed, tailgate, bumpers, and mirrors. Partial wraps focus on high-visibility areas like doors, tailgate, and bed sides. Lettering packages add your logo, phone number, and website at the lowest cost.

Pricing

Pickup truck wrap pricing depends on cab size, bed length, and coverage level:

  • Full wrap — $3,500–$4,500 (regular cab to crew cab)
  • Partial wrap — $1,500–$2,500
  • Lettering package — $350–$800
  • Tailgate only — $400–$700

Materials

We use only premium cast vinyl from Avery Dennison (MPI 1105) and 3M (IJ180-CV3) — rated for 5–7 years of outdoor durability. Cast vinyl conforms to truck body lines, rivets, and corrugations without lifting.

Fleet Truck Programs

Wrapping 5+ trucks? We offer fleet pricing, templated designs for brand consistency, and free pickup and delivery across Chicagoland. Same design, same quality, every truck.
