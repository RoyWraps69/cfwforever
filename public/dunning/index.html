Common Questions About Our Services

How long do vehicle wraps last?

Quality wraps using premium materials typically last 5-7 years with proper care. UV exposure, climate, and maintenance affect longevity. We use fade-resistant 3M and Avery Dennison materials rated for long-term outdoor exposure.

Can wraps be removed without damaging paint?

Yes. Professional removal by experienced installers ensures zero damage to original paint. We've removed thousands of wraps without incident, protecting your vehicle's resale value.

How much do vehicle wraps cost?

Pricing depends on vehicle size, design complexity, and material selection. Call (312) 597-1286 for a free, detailed quote. Most partial wraps range $1,500-$3,500; full wraps $2,500-$5,500+.

How long does installation take?

Most installations complete in 2-5 business days depending on complexity. Simple color change wraps may finish in 1-2 days. We schedule around your operational needs to minimize downtime.
