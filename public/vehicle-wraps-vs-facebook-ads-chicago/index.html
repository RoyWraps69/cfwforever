When Facebook Ads Are Worth It

Facebook ads are effective for specific campaign objectives: promoting a limited-time offer, targeting a precise demographic outside your service area, or driving traffic to a landing page. For brand awareness in your local service territory, they are a poor investment compared to vehicle wraps. The combination of both — a wrapped fleet plus targeted digital ads for specific offers — is the highest-performing strategy for local service businesses.
