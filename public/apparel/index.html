Frequently Asked Questions

Can Chicago Fleet Wraps design both my vehicle wraps and my team's apparel?

Yes, absolutely! We offer integrated design services to ensure seamless branding across both your fleet vehicles and your team's uniforms. Our designers will work to perfectly match your brand's colors and logos for a cohesive look.

What types of apparel can Chicago Fleet Wraps brand for my business?

We can brand a wide range of apparel, including polo shirts, t-shirts, jackets, hoodies, and workwear. We help you choose materials and styles best suited for your industry and the Chicago climate, ensuring durability and comfort.

How long does it take to get branded apparel and fleet wraps completed together?

The timeline varies based on design complexity and order volume. Typically, an integrated project, from design approval to installation and apparel delivery, can take 3-6 weeks. We prioritize efficiency to get your brand on the road and on your team quickly.

Yes, minimum order quantities for apparel typically apply, ranging from 12 to 24 pieces per item, depending on the garment type and branding method. This ensures cost-effectiveness and consistent quality for your team's uniforms.

How do you ensure color consistency between vehicle wraps and apparel?

Our design team uses precise color matching techniques, referencing your brand's specific Pantone codes or CMYK values. We also provide digital proofs and physical samples to ensure your brand colors are accurately represented on both your 3M-wrapped truck and your team's shirts.
