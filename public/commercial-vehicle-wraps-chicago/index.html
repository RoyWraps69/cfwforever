Frequently Asked Questions

What are commercial vehicle wraps?

Commercial vehicle wraps are branded vinyl graphics applied to business vehicles to advertise your company while driving and parked at job sites. They generate 30,000–70,000+ impressions per day per vehicle at just $0.04 CPM.

How much do commercial vehicle wraps cost?

Commercial wraps range from $2,200 for pickup trucks to $10,000+ for large box trucks. Fleet discounts of 5–15% apply for 3+ vehicles. All commercial wraps are 100% tax deductible. Full pricing →

How do you maintain brand consistency across a fleet?

We create a master design template that adapts to every vehicle type in your fleet — ensuring brand colors, logos, and messaging are consistent whether applied to a compact van or a 26-ft box truck.
