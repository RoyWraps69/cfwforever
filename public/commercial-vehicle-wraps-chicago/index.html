How long do commercial vehicle wraps last?

Commercial wraps using Avery Dennison MPI 1105 or 3M IJ180-CV3 cast vinyl are rated 5–7 years outdoors. With proper care, commercial fleet wraps regularly last 6–7 years even in Chicago's harsh winters.

Can commercial wraps be removed without damaging paint?

Yes. Zero verified paint damage claims in 10+ years. Professional removal by our certified installers preserves factory paint. Commercial wraps actually protect paint from UV, scratches, and road debris.
