Boat Wrap Care & Maintenance: Extending the Life of Your Chicago Vessel's Finish

Maintaining your boat wrap is straightforward and essential for preserving its vibrant appearance and protective qualities. Regular cleaning with mild soap and water, similar to how you'd wash your car, is highly recommended. Avoid abrasive cleaners or brushes that could scratch the vinyl. For stubborn grime or water spots, specialized marine vinyl cleaning products can be used.

To further extend the life of your boat wrap, particularly in Chicago's varying climate, consider applying a marine-grade wax or sealant specifically designed for vinyl surfaces. This adds an extra layer of UV protection and makes cleaning even easier. Proper care ensures that your wrap continues to look pristine, whether it's docked in Belmont Harbor or cruising past the Chicago skyline.
