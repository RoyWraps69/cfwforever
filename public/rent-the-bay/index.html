Frequently Asked Questions

What types of vehicles can I wrap in your rental bay?

Our bays are spacious enough to accommodate a wide range of vehicles, from compact cars and vans to larger box trucks and Sprinter vans. We recommend confirming your vehicle dimensions when booking to ensure we allocate the appropriate bay size.

Do you provide tools or do I need to bring my own for the bay rental?

While we provide the ideal environment, renters are generally expected to bring their own professional wrap installation tools. Basic cleaning supplies may be available, but specialized tools like heat guns, squeegees, and cutting instruments should be supplied by you.

Can I book a bay and have CFW staff assist with the installation?

Yes, for an additional fee, our experienced CFW installers can provide assistance or supervision during your bay rental. This is perfect for those who need an extra pair of hands or expert guidance on complex sections of their wrap project.

What is the typical turnaround time for your printing services?

Printing turnaround times vary based on the project's size and complexity, but typically range from 2-5 business days from final artwork approval. Rush options may be available for an additional charge; please inquire when submitting your design files.

Is your facility climate-controlled for optimal wrap installation?

Absolutely. Our rental bays are maintained at optimal temperature and humidity levels year-round, crucial for proper vinyl adhesion and preventing common installation issues like bubbling or premature lifting, ensuring a professional finish.

Do you offer design services if I only have an idea for my wrap?

While our primary focus is printing and bay rental, we can recommend trusted local graphic designers in the Chicago area who specialize in vehicle wrap layouts. They can help bring your vision to life before we handle the printing and provide the installation space.

What are your operating hours for bay rentals and print pickups?

Our standard operating hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Weekend or after-hours access for bay rentals may be available by special arrangement and could incur additional fees. Please contact us to discuss your specific scheduling needs.

What kind of warranty applies to printing services or materials purchased through CFW?

Our printing services utilize premium materials like Avery Dennison MPI 1105 and 3M IJ180-CV3, which come with manufacturer warranties against defects. For printing quality, we guarantee faithful reproduction of your approved artwork. Our 2-year workmanship warranty applies to wraps installed by CFW, not to self-installed wraps using our rental bay.
