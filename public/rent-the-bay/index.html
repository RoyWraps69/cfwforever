How much does it cost to rent a wrap bay in Chicago?

Chicago Fleet Wraps rents a professional, heated, climate-controlled wrap bay for $250 per day (8AM–6PM). The rental includes heat guns, propane torches, rolling scaffold, full squeegee kit, LED lighting, 110V & 220V power, WiFi, vehicle staging, and an optional vehicle lift. Bookings are for verified professional installers only. A 48-hour cancellation policy applies.
