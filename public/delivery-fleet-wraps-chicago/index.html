Understanding Delivery Fleet Wrap Costs and ROI

The investment in delivery fleet wraps varies based on vehicle size, coverage (full vs. partial), and design complexity. For a standard cargo van like a Ford Transit Connect, a partial wrap might range from $1,800 to $3,200, while a full wrap could be between $3,500 and $5,500. Larger box trucks or Sprinter vans will naturally fall into higher price brackets, typically from $4,500 to $8,000+ for full coverage, reflecting the increased material and labor required.

When evaluating the return on investment (ROI), consider the long-term advertising value. Compared to monthly billboard rentals costing thousands, a durable vehicle wrap offers years of advertising for a one-time fee. The constant exposure your delivery vehicles generate across Chicago's expressways and local streets translates directly into increased brand recall and customer inquiries, providing a demonstrably higher ROI for businesses committed to sustained visibility.
