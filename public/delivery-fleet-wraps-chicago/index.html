Frequently Asked Questions

How much do delivery fleet wraps cost in Chicago?

Delivery van wraps start at $2,800–$4,500 for cargo vans, $3,500–$5,200 for sprinters, and $4,200–$10,000 for box trucks. Fleet discounts of 5–15% apply for 3+ vehicles.

Can you wrap high-mileage delivery vehicles?

Absolutely. We routinely wrap delivery vehicles logging 50,000–100,000+ miles per year. Our cast vinyl withstands highway driving, urban stop-and-go, and loading dock contact.

How fast can you wrap a delivery fleet?

We wrap 1–3 vehicles at a time to minimize fleet downtime. A 10-vehicle fleet typically completes in 4–6 weeks. Rush scheduling is available.
