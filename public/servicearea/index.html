Free Fleet Pickup & Delivery Program

Downtime costs money. That is why we built our pickup and delivery program to minimize the time your vehicles spend off the road. Here is exactly how it works:

  1. Request a pickup — Call (312) 597-1286 or submit an online estimate. Tell us how many vehicles, what type, and your preferred pickup window.
  2. We schedule around your fleet — Early morning (6 AM), evening, and weekend pickups available. We work around your dispatch schedule so trucks are never pulled during peak hours.
  3. Our driver picks up your vehicle — A fully insured driver arrives at your yard, lot, or jobsite. We photograph the vehicle's condition before transport.
  4. Wrap installation at our shop — Your vehicle is wrapped in our climate-controlled, 70°F facility under LED lighting for perfect adhesion and color accuracy. Typical turnaround: 1–3 business days depending on vehicle size.
  5. Quality inspection & photos — Every wrap is inspected by a second technician. We photograph the completed wrap from every angle and send you a preview before delivery.
  6. Free return delivery — We deliver the wrapped vehicle back to your location, clean and ready to hit the road. You receive care instructions and your 2-year warranty card.

For fleets of 5+ vehicles, we stagger pickups so you never lose more than one or two trucks from service at a time. We have wrapped entire fleets of 30+ vehicles with zero missed routes.
