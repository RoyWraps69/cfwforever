Car wrap Chicago — Chicago Fleet Wraps has been installing car wraps, color change wraps, and custom vehicle graphics in Chicago since 2014. Car Wrap Options in Chicago

Matte Finish Wraps

Matte black, grey, white, or color. Factory paint can't achieve a true matte finish — vinyl can. Avery Dennison's Supreme Wrapping Film matte series is our most-requested color change product.

Satin Finishes

Between gloss and matte, satin wraps have a soft sheen that reads differently in different lighting. Popular for luxury and performance vehicles throughout Chicago and the northern suburbs.

Chrome Delete

Replace chrome trim, grille surrounds, mirror caps, and door handles with black or body-color vinyl. One of the fastest-growing requests from Chicago car owners wanting a blacked-out aesthetic.

Custom Commercial Graphics

Full custom design, partial wraps, decals, and lettering. Design and install in-house — no subcontracting. Everything done at our Lamon Ave facility.
