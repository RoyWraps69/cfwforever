Car Wrap Chicago

Color change wraps, matte finishes, satin, chrome delete, custom graphics. Cast vinyl only.

Chicago's Car Wrap Shop — 4711 N Lamon Ave

Car wrap Chicago — Chicago Fleet Wraps has been installing car wraps, color change wraps, and custom vehicle graphics in Chicago since 2001. Our facility on Lamon Ave in Portage Park is climate-controlled year-round, which matters in Chicago's extreme weather. Vinyl applied in January in an unheated garage fails. Vinyl applied at the correct temperature in a controlled environment lasts 5-7 years.

We use cast vinyl only — Avery Dennison MPI 1105 and 3M IJ180-CV3. Not calendered vinyl. Not import film. Cast vinyl conforms to complex body contours, holds color under UV exposure, and removes cleanly when the time comes. For car wraps Chicago car enthusiasts and commercial clients both depend on, the material choice is not negotiable.

Vehicle wraps Chicago-wide — we serve every neighborhood from Rogers Park to Beverly, from the Gold Coast to Austin. For color change car wraps, we draw clients from Lincoln Park, Wicker Park, Bucktown, the Near North Side, and throughout the western and northern suburbs including Oak Park, Evanston, Skokie, Schaumburg, and Naperville.

Car Wrap Options in Chicago

Matte Finish Wraps

Matte black, matte grey, matte white, matte color. The most popular color change category for Chicago car wraps. Factory paint can't achieve a true matte finish — vinyl can. Avery Dennison's Supreme Wrapping Film matte series is our most-requested color change product.

Satin Finishes

Between gloss and matte, satin wraps have a soft sheen that reads differently in different lighting. Satin grey on a darker vehicle picks up ambient light in a way matte doesn't. Popular for luxury and performance vehicles throughout Chicago and the northern suburbs.

Chrome Delete

Replace chrome trim, grille surrounds, mirror caps, and door handles with black or body-color vinyl. Chrome delete is one of the fastest-growing requests we get from Chicago car owners who want a blacked-out aesthetic without painting chrome parts.

Custom Graphics and Commercial

Full custom design, partial wraps, decals, and lettering. Whether it's a personal vehicle or a commercial car wrap Chicago business use, we design and install in-house. No subcontracting, no outsourced design — everything done at our Lamon Ave facility.

Car Wrap Pricing in Chicago

Car wrap cost in Chicago depends on vehicle size, wrap coverage, and vinyl type. Chicago Fleet Wraps pricing for full car wraps using cast vinyl:

Compact cars — $2,500 to $3,000. Two-door coupes and small sedans have less surface area and simpler body lines.

Mid-size sedans and sports cars — $3,000 to $3,800. Four-door sedans, coupes with complex body lines, and performance vehicles with aggressive body kits take more time and material.

SUVs and trucks — $3,200 to $4,500. More surface area, higher rooflines, and more complex body lines.

Partial wraps and accents — $800 to $2,000. Hood, roof, pillars, mirrors — any combination of panels can be wrapped without doing the full vehicle.

All pricing includes cast vinyl material, surface prep, installation, and a 2-year workmanship warranty. Use our vehicle wrap cost calculator for an instant estimate on your specific vehicle.
