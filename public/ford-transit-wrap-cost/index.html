What Determines Ford Transit Wrap Cost

Ford Transit wrap cost is primarily driven by roof height and wheelbase. Standard roof models are the most affordable to wrap because they have less surface area and the panels are accessible without step equipment. High-roof models add $400–$1,100 to the base price. Extended wheelbase models add $150–$300 over standard length equivalents.
