Essential Maintenance & Longevity for Your Wrapped Chicago Fleet

To ensure your Chicago fleet wraps maintain their vibrant appearance and structural integrity for years, proper maintenance is key. We recommend regular hand washing with mild soap and water, avoiding abrasive brushes or high-pressure washers that can damage the vinyl. For stubborn grime accumulated from driving through areas like the Illinois Tollway system or construction zones, specialized wrap cleaning solutions are available and highly effective. Promptly removing bird droppings or tree sap is crucial to prevent etching into the wrap material.

Our premium Avery Dennison MPI 1105 and 3M IJ180-CV3 cast vinyl films are designed for exceptional durability, offering a lifespan of 5-7 years with proper care. This longevity, combined with our 2-year workmanship warranty, means your fleet's mobile advertising remains impactful and professional for a significant period. By following these simple maintenance guidelines, your wrapped vehicles will continue to make a powerful statement across Chicago's diverse neighborhoods, from Wicker Park to Beverly, for the duration of their service.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical lead time for a full fleet wrap program with Chicago Fleet Wraps?

Once designs are approved, production and installation for a single vehicle typically takes 3-5 business days. For a fleet of 5-10 vehicles, expect a staggered timeline of 2-3 weeks, depending on vehicle availability and design complexity. We coordinate closely to minimize your downtime.

Can Chicago Fleet Wraps handle a mix of different vehicle types within a single fleet program?

Absolutely. We specialize in wrapping diverse fleets, from Ford Transit vans and Mercedes-Benz Sprinters to box trucks and EV delivery vehicles. Our design team creates tailored templates for each model, ensuring consistent branding across your entire Chicago fleet.

Do you offer volume discounts for larger fleet wrap projects in Chicagoland?

Yes, we provide competitive volume-based pricing for businesses looking to wrap multiple vehicles. The exact discount depends on the total number of vehicles and the scope of work. Contact us for a personalized quote for your Chicago-based fleet.

What happens if a wrapped vehicle in my fleet gets damaged in an accident?

In the unfortunate event of damage, we can facilitate partial re-wraps. Our team will assess the damaged sections and print/install replacement panels, ensuring your brand consistency is quickly restored. This is far more cost-effective than repainting.

How long will the premium cast vinyl wraps last on my Chicago fleet vehicles?

With our exclusive use of Avery Dennison MPI 1105 and 3M IJ180-CV3 cast vinyl, your fleet wraps are designed to last 5-7 years. This durability ensures a long-lasting mobile advertisement, even with Chicago's varying weather conditions.

Can I update the branding on my fleet wraps after a few years?

Yes, one of the key advantages of fleet wraps is their flexibility. If your branding evolves, we can easily design and install new wraps or partial updates, refreshing your fleet's appearance without the need for expensive repaints. This keeps your Chicago fleet current and impactful.

What types of businesses in Chicago benefit most from a fleet wrap program?

Businesses with service vehicles that frequently travel across Chicago and its suburbs, such as HVAC, plumbing, electrical, landscaping, delivery, construction, and food trucks, benefit immensely. Any business seeking high-impact, continuous brand exposure will see significant returns.
