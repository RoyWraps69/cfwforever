Chicago's Premier Vehicle Wrap Shop

Chicago Fleet Wraps has completed over 169 documented vehicle wrap projects across the greater Chicago metropolitan area, serving businesses of all sizes from solo operators to enterprise fleets. Our portfolio spans every major vehicle category — from Ford Transit cargo vans and Mercedes-Benz Sprinters to Rivian R1T electric pickups, Freightliner box trucks, and custom boat wraps.

Every wrap in this portfolio was designed, printed, and installed in-house at our Chicago facility. We use only 3M and Avery Dennison cast vinyl films, ensuring wraps that last 5–7 years in Chicago's extreme weather conditions — from -20°F winters to 100°F summers.

What Vehicle Types Do We Wrap?

  • Cargo Vans — Ford Transit, Ram ProMaster, Chevy Express, GMC Savana
  • Sprinter Vans — Mercedes-Benz Sprinter, Ford Transit High Roof
  • Box Trucks — 16 ft, 20 ft, 24 ft, 26 ft Isuzu, Hino, Freightliner
  • Pickup Trucks — Ford F-150/F-250, Ram 1500/2500, Chevy Silverado, GMC Sierra
  • Electric Vehicles — Rivian R1T, Rivian R1S, Tesla Model 3/Y/X, Amazon Rivian EDV
  • Cars & Sedans — Full color change wraps, partial wraps, racing stripes
  • SUVs — Color change, fleet graphics, partial wraps
  • Semi Trucks — Cab wraps, trailer wraps, full rig graphics
  • Boats — Hull wraps, deck graphics, full boat wraps
  • Wall Wraps — Office murals, retail graphics, event backdrops

Notable Clients in Our Portfolio

Our portfolio includes wraps for Precision Today, Arnold Electric, Blue Origin, State Farm, MH Equipment, Improovy Painters, OAKBROS, 1800 Tequila, Patron, Amazon, Boeing, and dozens of small businesses across Chicago, Schaumburg, Naperville, Aurora, Joliet, and the surrounding suburbs.
