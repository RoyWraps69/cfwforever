After the Wrap — Care, Warranty, and Reorders

Every Isuzu NPR / NPR HD wrap includes a 2-year workmanship warranty, which covers issues like lifting, bubbling, edge peeling, and any installation-related defects. This is distinct from the manufacturer's vinyl warranty; both Avery Dennison and 3M provide their own film performance warranties, typically ranging from 5–7 years for outdoor use on commercial vehicles like the Isuzu NPR.

To maximize the lifespan of your Isuzu NPR / NPR HD wrap, always hand wash it with mild soap and water. Avoid automated brush car washes as they can scratch the vinyl and lift edges. When using pressure washers, keep the PSI below 1,200 and maintain a distance of at least 12 inches from panel edges. Promptly remove bird droppings and tree sap, as their acidic compounds can etch into the vinyl if left for extended periods.
