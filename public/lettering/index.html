  • Fleet identification — unit numbers, vehicle IDs, and internal tracking
  • Regulatory text — USDOT, GVW, and required commercial markings
  • Logo decals — full-color printed and die-cut logo graphics

Cut Vinyl vs. Printed Graphics

Cut vinyl lettering uses solid-color vinyl cut into individual letters and shapes. It's clean, professional, and extremely durable — lasting 7–10 years outdoors. For simple text and single-color logos, cut vinyl is the most cost-effective option.

Printed graphics allow full-color logos, photos, and complex designs. We print on premium cast vinyl with UV overlaminate for 5–7 years of outdoor durability.
