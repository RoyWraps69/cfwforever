Maintenance Tips for Extending the Life of Your Partial Wrap in Chicago

Proper maintenance is key to preserving the vibrant appearance and longevity of your partial vehicle wrap, particularly given Chicago's varied climate. We recommend hand washing your wrapped vehicle with a mild detergent and soft cloth, avoiding abrasive brushes or high-pressure washes directly on the edges of the wrap. Regular cleaning helps prevent the buildup of grime and pollutants commonly found on city streets, like those around the Dan Ryan Expressway.

For stubborn spots, use a specialized wrap cleaner. Avoid petroleum-based solvents, which can damage the vinyl. Parking in shaded areas or garages when possible will also help protect your wrap from prolonged exposure to UV rays, further extending its life and maintaining its color integrity. Following these simple guidelines will ensure your partial wrap continues to effectively promote your business throughout its impressive 5-7 year lifespan.
