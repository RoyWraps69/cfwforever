The DSP Wrap Process

Amazon DSP operators face a unique challenge: you need branded vehicles but cannot afford significant downtime. Our DSP program solves this by phasing installations — typically 2–4 vehicles per day — so the majority of your fleet stays on route throughout the wrap program.

For a 20-vehicle fleet, we typically complete the program in 8–12 business days. For 50+ vehicle fleets, we develop a custom phasing schedule that integrates with your dispatch calendar.

  1. Fleet audit — we catalog every vehicle in your DSP program by configuration and condition
  2. Design approval — brand standard-compliant designs created and approved before production
  3. Phased pickup schedule — vehicles pulled in batches that match your operational needs
  4. Installation and QC — each vehicle inspected by our lead installer before delivery
  5. Fleet account setup — we maintain your files for future new vehicles

Amazon DSP Wrap FAQs

Do you need to comply with Amazon brand standards?

We are familiar with Amazon DSP brand standard requirements and work with operators to ensure compliance. Non-compliance with Amazon brand standards can affect your DSP standing — we take this seriously and flag any potential compliance issues during the design process.

How quickly can you wrap our entire DSP fleet?

For a 20-vehicle DSP fleet, we typically complete the program in 8–12 business days with phased scheduling. For larger fleets, we develop a custom schedule. Contact us with your fleet size and we will give you a specific timeline.

Can you handle new vehicle additions as our fleet grows?

Yes — we maintain fleet accounts for all DSP programs. When you add vehicles, we have your approved designs on file and can schedule new installs within days. New vehicles match the existing fleet exactly because we never discard your files.
