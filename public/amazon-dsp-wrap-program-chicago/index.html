Frequently Asked Questions

How do you handle a fleet with multiple vehicle types?

We adapt your approved brand standard to every vehicle template in your fleet. Colors are Pantone-matched across every print run. A Transit van and a box truck carry the same brand identity with graphics scaled and repositioned to suit each vehicle's proportions.

What happens when vehicles are retired from the fleet?

We offer professional wrap removal as a separate service. Cast vinyl removes cleanly, leaving factory paint intact and typically in better condition than an unwrapped vehicle of the same age.

Can you handle rush jobs?

Yes — rush jobs are available with advance notice. We have turned around single-vehicle wraps in 48 hours when the schedule allows. Call (312) 597-1286 to discuss your timeline.
