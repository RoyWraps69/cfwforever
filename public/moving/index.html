Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a moving truck wrap cost?

Box truck wraps range from $4,200–$10,000 depending on truck size. Cargo van wraps start at $2,800. Fleet discounts of 5–15% apply for 3+ vehicles. All wraps are 100% tax deductible under IRS Section 179.

Are moving truck wraps worth the investment?

Absolutely. A wrapped 26-ft moving truck generates 70,000+ daily impressions on Chicago expressways. Customers consistently choose branded movers over unmarked trucks — leading to higher booking rates and premium pricing.

Can you wrap leased moving trucks?

Yes. Vinyl wraps are non-permanent and can be professionally removed without damaging paint. Many moving companies wrap leased box trucks and return them in perfect condition at lease end.

How long does installation take for a moving fleet?

Individual box trucks take 2–4 business days. For multi-truck fleets, we stagger installation to keep your fleet operational — typically wrapping 1–2 trucks at a time while the rest continue working.
