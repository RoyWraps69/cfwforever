Why Chicago Businesses Choose Fleet Wraps

Chicago is home to over 2.7 million residents and hundreds of thousands of daily commuters — making it one of the highest-traffic metro areas in the country. A single wrapped cargo van driving through neighborhoods like Lincoln Park, Bucktown, or Hyde Park generates an estimated 30,000–70,000 daily impressions. For businesses competing in Chicago's dense commercial landscape, fleet wraps deliver the lowest cost-per-impression of any advertising medium — often under $0.04 CPM compared to $15–$25 for digital ads.

Chicago's service economy is massive. When a homeowner in Lincoln Square sees a professionally wrapped van parked on their street, it creates instant credibility that no Google Ad can replicate.

Frequently Asked Questions — Chicago Fleet Wraps

How long does it take to wrap a fleet vehicle in Chicago?

Cargo vans typically take 1–2 business days, Sprinter vans 2–3 days, and box trucks 2–4 days depending on size. We phase fleet orders to minimize downtime — most Chicago businesses have vehicles back on the road within the week.

Do you pick up vehicles from my Chicago location?

Yes — we offer free fleet pickup and delivery anywhere in Chicago. Whether your vehicles are parked in a West Loop garage, a South Side yard, or a North Side lot, our team will pick them up and return them wrapped and ready to drive.

What materials do you use for Chicago fleet wraps?

We use Avery Dennison MPI 1105 with DOL 1360 overlaminate and 3M IJ180-CV3 with 8548G overlaminate — both premium cast vinyl films rated for 5–7 years of outdoor durability. These materials withstand Chicago's freeze-thaw cycles, road salt, and intense summer sun.
