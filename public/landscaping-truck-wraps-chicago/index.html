Why Landscaping Companies Need Truck Wraps

Landscaping trucks and trailers are visible in residential neighborhoods 8–10 hours a day during the growing season. Every homeowner watching you transform their neighbor's yard is a potential customer. A professionally wrapped truck with your company name, phone number, and services tells the neighborhood you're an established, professional operation — not a weekend warrior with a push mower.

Chicago's landscaping season runs April through November, with snow removal extending many companies year-round. A wrapped fleet generates consistent leads across seasons: lawn care inquiries in spring, hardscape and patio projects in summer, cleanups in fall, and snow removal in winter. At $0.04 CPM, wraps cost a fraction of door-to-door flyers or digital ads.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do landscaping truck wraps cost in Chicago?

Pickup truck wraps start at $2,200–$3,800. Cargo van wraps start at $2,800–$4,500. Trailer wraps start at $1,500–$3,500. Fleet discounts of 5–15% apply for 3+ vehicles.

Can you wrap landscaping trailers?

Yes. We wrap enclosed trailers, open trailer side panels, and tailgates. Trailer lettering starts at $400 and full trailer wraps start at $1,500–$3,500 depending on size.

How long do landscaping truck wraps last?

Our wraps use premium cast vinyl rated for 5–7 years. Landscaping vehicles take extra abuse from dirt and debris, so we use heavy-duty overlaminate for maximum protection. Includes a 2-year installation warranty.
