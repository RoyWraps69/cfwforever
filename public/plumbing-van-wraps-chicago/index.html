Why Plumbing Companies Need Van Wraps

Plumbing emergencies create the highest-intent moments in home services. When a neighbor sees your branded van in a driveway during a flooding emergency, they're making a mental note for when they need a plumber. A professionally wrapped plumbing van generates 30,000–70,000 daily impressions at $0.04 CPM — a fraction of what plumbing companies spend on Google Local Service Ads ($25–$75 per lead).

Chicago's aging housing stock (average home age: 67 years) means constant demand for plumbing services. Older galvanized pipes, cast iron sewer lines, and aging water heaters keep plumbing vans busy year-round. Every service call is a branding opportunity in neighborhoods where word-of-mouth drives most plumbing referrals.
