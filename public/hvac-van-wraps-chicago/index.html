Why HVAC Companies Need Van Wraps

HVAC service vans are uniquely positioned for maximum advertising impact. Unlike delivery vehicles that stop briefly, HVAC vans park in residential driveways for 2–8 hours during installations and service calls. Every neighbor walking their dog, every parent on the school run, every mail carrier sees your brand — and remembers it when their furnace breaks down in January or their AC fails in July.

Chicago's extreme seasons drive year-round HVAC demand. A professionally wrapped van generates 30,000–70,000 daily impressions at just $0.04 CPM — compared to $3.50+ CPM for Google Ads and $8–$25 per lead on Angi or HomeAdvisor. One wrapped van pays for itself with a single job referral.

HVAC Van Wrap Design Best Practices

The most effective HVAC van wraps follow a clear hierarchy: company name visible from 100+ feet, phone number readable from 50 feet, and services listed for close-range reading. We design wraps that work from every angle — crucial because HVAC vans are often viewed from the side while parked in driveways.
