Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical timeline for wrapping an HVAC service van at Chicago Fleet Wraps?

For a full HVAC van wrap, the installation process usually takes 2-3 business days per vehicle. This includes meticulous cleaning, precise application of the cast vinyl, and a thorough quality inspection. Our team works efficiently to get your essential service vehicles back on the road in Chicago and its suburbs quickly.

How do your HVAC van wraps withstand Chicago's extreme weather conditions?

Our HVAC van wraps are engineered to endure Chicago's harsh climate, from icy winters to humid summers. We exclusively use Avery Dennison MPI 1105 and 3M IJ180-CV3 cast vinyl, which are designed for extreme durability, UV resistance, and flexibility, ensuring your wrap remains vibrant and intact for years.

Can you match my existing HVAC company's branding and color schemes precisely?

. Our design team specializes in precise color matching and brand integration. We work directly with your existing brand guidelines to ensure your HVAC van wraps perfectly reflect your company's established identity and messaging, providing a consistent look across your entire fleet in Chicagoland.

What is the expected lifespan of an HVAC van wrap from Chicago Fleet Wraps?

With our premium materials and expert installation, you can expect your HVAC van wrap to maintain its professional appearance and integrity for 5-7 years, often longer with proper care. We also back our workmanship with a 2-year warranty, giving Chicago HVAC businesses peace of mind.

Do you offer partial wrap options for HVAC vans, and how do they compare in cost?

Yes, we offer partial wrap solutions for HVAC vans, which can be a cost-effective way to brand your fleet. Partial wraps typically range from $2,200-$3,500, offering significant visual impact by strategically integrating with your vehicle's base paint, while full wraps range from $3,800-$5,500.

Will wrapping my HVAC van damage the original paint, and what about removal?

No, our premium cast vinyl wraps actually protect your vehicle's original paint from scratches, UV exposure, and minor abrasions. When professionally removed by Chicago Fleet Wraps, the wrap will not damage the underlying paint, leaving it in its original condition, preserving your vehicle's resale value.

Can I include specific services like 'Furnace Repair' or 'AC Installation' on my HVAC van wrap?

Definitely! We encourage HVAC companies to prominently feature their key services, contact information, and even special offers directly on their van wraps. This ensures that every time your vehicle is seen in Chicago neighborhoods like Lakeview or Roscoe Village, potential customers immediately understand what you offer.

Do you offer fleet services for multiple HVAC vans in the Chicago area?

Yes, Chicago Fleet Wraps specializes in fleet services for HVAC companies of all sizes. We can coordinate wrapping multiple vans, ensuring consistent branding and efficient project management. We even offer free fleet pickup across Chicagoland to simplify the process for your business.
