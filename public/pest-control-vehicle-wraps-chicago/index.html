Why Pest Control Companies Wrap Their Vehicles in Chicago

Pest control vehicles park in front of homes and businesses for 30–90 minutes per job. In Chicago's dense residential neighborhoods — Lincoln Square, Bridgeport, Beverly — that's guaranteed exposure to hundreds of potential customers per day. Independent pest control companies use wraps to compete visually with Orkin, Terminix, and Rollins fleets.

What to Include on a Pest Control Vehicle Wrap

  • Company name and logo — recognizable, professional
  • Phone number — large format, both sides
  • Services — ants, rodents, bed bugs, termites, mosquitoes
  • License number — required for pest control operators in Illinois
  • Website and QR code — drives online bookings

Illinois Pest Control Licensing on Vehicle Wraps

Illinois Department of Agriculture requires licensed pest control operators to display their business name and IDOA license number on commercial vehicles. We incorporate compliance requirements into every pest control wrap design at no additional charge.

Do pest control wraps need to show licensing information?

Yes. Illinois requires your IDOA pest control operator license number on commercial vehicles. We include this in the design at no extra cost.

How do pest control wraps hold up to chemical exposure?
