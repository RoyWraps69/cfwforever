Lettering vs. Partial vs. Full Van Wraps

Vinyl lettering ($400–$1,200) covers the basics — company name, phone number, website, and license info. Best for businesses just starting out or meeting DOT requirements.

Partial van wraps ($1,200–$2,800) add visual impact with graphics on key panels — sides, rear doors, and hood. Partial wraps deliver 60–80% of the visual impact at 40–60% of full wrap cost. Partial wraps →

Full van wraps ($2,800–$5,200) maximize impressions with complete coverage. Full wraps create the strongest brand presence and generate the most leads per vehicle.

Frequently Asked Questions About Van Wraps

How much do van wraps cost in Chicago?

Van wrap pricing depends on van size and coverage. Cargo van wraps cost $2,800–$4,500, sprinter van wraps $3,500–$5,200, and compact van wraps $2,200–$3,500. Partial van wraps start at $1,200. Fleet discounts of 5–15% apply for 3+ vans.

What are the best industries for van wraps?

HVAC, plumbing, electrical, cleaning services, mobile detailing, delivery companies, pest control, and any service business that dispatches vans to residential and commercial job sites daily.

Lettering vs partial vs full van wrap — which should I choose?

Vinyl lettering ($400–$1,200) covers the basics. Partial van wraps ($1,200–$2,800) add visual impact at 40–60% of full wrap cost. Full van wraps ($2,800–$5,200) maximize impressions and deliver the strongest brand presence.

What is the ROI of a van wrap?

A wrapped van generates 30,000–70,000+ daily impressions at $0.04 CPM. Over 5 years, a $3,500 van wrap delivers 2,500%+ ROI. Use our ROI calculator to estimate your return.

How long does a van wrap last?

Van wraps with Avery Dennison MPI 1105 or 3M IJ180-CV3 cast vinyl are rated 5–7 years outdoors. With proper care, van wraps regularly last 6–7 years in Chicago weather.

Will a van wrap damage my van's paint?

No. Zero verified paint damage claims in 10+ years. Cast vinyl van wraps protect factory paint from UV, scratches, and road debris.

How long does van wrap installation take?

Compact vans 1–2 days, cargo vans 1–2 days, sprinter/transit vans 2–3 days. Free pickup and delivery across Chicagoland.

Can I wrap my van myself?

Professional installation is strongly recommended. Van wrapping requires specialized tools, climate-controlled conditions, and experience with complex curves and seams. DIY van wraps often result in bubbles, lifting, and premature failure.

Do you offer van fleet wraps?

Yes — van fleet wraps are our specialty. Volume discounts of 5–15% for fleets of 3+ vans. Learn about fleet wraps →

Can van wraps be removed or updated?

Yes. Van wraps can be professionally removed without damaging paint. Removal typically takes 4–6 hours. We can remove and replace for rebrands or design updates.
