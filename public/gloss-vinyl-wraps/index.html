Options & Finishes

Maintaining Your Gloss Wrap's Brilliant Finish in Chicago's Climate

To ensure your gloss vinyl wrap maintains its showroom-quality shine amidst Chicago's diverse weather conditions, proper care is essential. We recommend regular hand washing with mild, pH-neutral soap and water, avoiding abrasive brushes or high-pressure washers, especially near wrap edges. This prevents the buildup of road grime and pollutants, preserving the vibrant, glossy appearance of your Avery Dennison or 3M film.

For stubborn spots or environmental fallout, specialized wrap cleaning solutions can be used. Promptly removing bird droppings or tree sap is crucial to prevent staining. With diligent maintenance, your gloss wrap will continue to turn heads from Navy Pier to O'Hare, projecting a professional and polished image for your business and protecting the underlying vehicle paint, all backed by our 2-year workmanship warranty.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a gloss vinyl wrap different from other finishes like matte or satin?

A gloss wrap provides a reflective, paint-like shine, offering a vibrant and polished appearance that enhances color depth. Matte wraps have a dull, non-reflective finish, while satin offers a subtle sheen, a middle ground between gloss and matte. Gloss is ideal for maximum visual pop.

Can I get a custom color gloss wrap for my specific brand colors in Chicago?

Absolutely! We can match almost any custom brand color with our premium Avery Dennison MPI 1105 and 3M IJ180-CV3 films, ensuring your gloss wrap perfectly represents your corporate identity, whether for a single vehicle or an entire fleet operating in downtown Chicago.

How long does a typical full gloss wrap installation take at your Chicago facility?

A full gloss wrap installation for a standard commercial vehicle, such as a Ford F-150 or a Nissan NV, typically takes 2-4 days at our 4711 N Lamon Ave #7 location. This includes prep work and a meticulous application process to ensure a flawless, durable finish.

What is the expected lifespan of a gloss vinyl wrap in Chicago's climate?

With proper care and maintenance, our premium gloss wraps using Avery Dennison and 3M materials are designed to last 5-7 years, even with Chicago's varying weather. Our 2-year workmanship warranty further ensures your investment is protected.

Will a gloss wrap protect my vehicle's original paint from scratches and UV damage?

Yes, a gloss vinyl wrap acts as a protective layer, shielding your vehicle's original paint from minor abrasions, stone chips, and harmful UV rays, which helps preserve its resale value. We have zero verified paint damage claims upon removal.

What's the cost range for a full gloss wrap on a commercial van in the Chicago area?

For a full gloss wrap on a commercial van like a Ram ProMaster or a Ford Transit, clients in Chicago can expect to invest between $3,500 and $6,500. This includes premium materials and expert installation, providing a high ROI marketing asset.

Can I get a gloss wrap for just a portion of my vehicle, like the hood or side panels?

Yes, we offer partial gloss wraps, which are an excellent way to add high-impact branding to specific sections of your vehicle. These typically range from $1,800 to $3,800, depending on the area covered and design complexity, perfect for targeted messaging.
