Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a commercial vehicle wrap last?

Avery Dennison MPI 1105 and 3M IJ180-CV3 are rated 5–7 years outdoor. With proper care — hand washing, no brush car washes — Chicago fleet wraps regularly last 6–7 years even with road salt and winter exposure.

Will a wrap damage my vehicle's paint?

No. Chicago Fleet Wraps has zero verified paint damage claims in 10+ years. Wraps protect factory paint from UV and minor abrasion. When removed correctly, paint is preserved — often better than unwrapped vehicles of the same age.

Are vehicle wraps tax deductible?

Yes. Commercial vehicle wraps are 100% deductible under IRS Section 179 as a business advertising expense. Consult your tax professional for specifics.
