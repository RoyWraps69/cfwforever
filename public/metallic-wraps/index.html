This unique characteristic ensures your wrapped vehicles, whether a Ford Transit Connect or a Ram ProMaster, stand out on expressways like I-90 or Lake Shore Drive.

At Chicago Fleet Wraps, we exclusively use top-tier cast vinyl films such as Avery Dennison MPI 1105 and 3M IJ180-CV3 for our metallic applications. Maintaining Your Metallic Shine: Care Tips for Longevity

To ensure your metallic wrap maintains its dazzling appearance and extends its lifespan, proper care is essential. Regular hand washing with mild, pH-neutral soap and water is recommended, avoiding abrasive brushes or high-pressure washers that can damage the vinyl. For stubborn grime accumulated from driving on the Eisenhower Expressway or through construction zones, specialized vinyl cleaning solutions can be used.

Avoid parking under direct sunlight for prolonged periods when possible, especially during peak summer months, as excessive UV exposure can eventually diminish the metallic luster. While our premium films are UV resistant, mindful parking helps preserve their vibrancy. With consistent care, your metallic wrap from Chicago Fleet Wraps will continue to turn heads and effectively market your business throughout its impressive lifespan, protecting your vehicle's original paint underneath.
