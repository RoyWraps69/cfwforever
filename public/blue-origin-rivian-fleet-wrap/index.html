Fleet Services

Fleet Wrap Design Philosophy

At Chicago Fleet Wraps, we approach every fleet design as a brand engineering challenge, not just a graphic design project. The goal isn't to make a pretty vehicle — it's to create a mobile advertising system that generates maximum brand recognition and recall in the specific markets where our clients operate.

Our design process starts with three questions: Who is your target customer? Where do they see your vehicles? What action do you want them to take? The answers shape every design decision — from color palette and typography to information hierarchy and call-to-action placement. A plumbing company serving residential neighborhoods needs a different design approach than a logistics company serving commercial districts.

We design for readability at three distances: 50+ feet (brand recognition — logo, colors, and vehicle silhouette), 20–50 feet (service identification — what you do), and under 20 feet (contact information — phone number, website, QR code). This layered approach ensures your wrap communicates effectively whether it's driving past at 45 mph or parked on a job site. Every element is tested against these distance thresholds before final approval.
