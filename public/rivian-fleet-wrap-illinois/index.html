Why Rivian Requires Specialized Installation

Rivian's design philosophy creates installation challenges that most vinyl wrap shops have never encountered. The stadium headlight assembly wraps around the front corner — applying vinyl here without bridging or lifting requires precision heat work and multi-section application. The R1T's gear tunnel creates a surface that must be addressed as a separate wrap zone. The R1S's rear liftgate with integrated taillights requires graphic planning to account for the light bar.

After 600+ Rivian installations, our team knows every model year variation. When Rivian updated the R1T in 2024 with revised front fascia geometry, we adapted our installation process within the first few vehicles. That kind of hands-on model knowledge does not exist at shops that wrap one or two Rivians a year.

Rivian Wrap FAQs

Does wrapping a Rivian void the warranty?

No. The Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act prevents Rivian from voiding your warranty solely because you wrapped the vehicle. Chicago Fleet Wraps has zero paint damage claims across 600+ Rivian installations. We have never damaged a Rivian's paint or affected any vehicle system through wrapping.

What makes Rivian installation different from other vehicles?

Rivian's stadium headlights, gear tunnel (R1T), and complex rear treatments require installation techniques not covered by standard training. After 600+ Rivian wraps, our installers know every quirk of every model year — the front corner work, the gear tunnel treatment, and the rear liftgate on the R1S.

Can you wrap a Rivian R1T for a business fleet?

Yes — commercial fleet graphics on the Rivian R1T are available. Contractors, landscapers, and trade businesses operating R1T fleets use our commercial wrap service to brand their vehicles consistently. Fleet discounts apply for multiple Rivian units.
