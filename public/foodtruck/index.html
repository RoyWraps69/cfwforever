Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a food truck wrap cost?

Food truck full wraps range from $4,200–$8,000 depending on truck size, design complexity, and number of serving windows. Partial wraps start at $1,500. Menu board graphics start at $400.

Are food truck wraps food-safe?

Yes. All food truck wraps use FDA-compliant, food-safe overlaminate. We use heat-resistant adhesive for surfaces near grills, fryers, and steam — ensuring the wrap stays in place and doesn't contaminate food contact areas.

How long does a food truck wrap last?

Our food truck wraps use premium cast vinyl rated for 5–7 years of outdoor durability. The overlaminate protects against grease splatter, steam, and UV exposure. Regular cleaning extends wrap life significantly.

Can you wrap around serving windows and equipment?

Yes. We template every food truck individually to account for serving windows, exhaust vents, generators, and equipment. The wrap is precision-cut to fit around every component for a seamless, professional finish.
