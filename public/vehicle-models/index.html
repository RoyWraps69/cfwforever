Maintaining Your Wrapped Vehicle for Lasting Impact in Chicago

Proper maintenance is key to preserving the vibrant appearance and longevity of your commercial vehicle wrap amidst Chicago's varied weather conditions. We recommend hand washing your wrapped vehicles regularly using mild, non-abrasive soap and water.

Avoid automated car washes, especially those with brushes, as these can damage the vinyl and compromise the wrap's integrity. High-pressure sprays should also be used with caution, keeping the nozzle at a safe distance.

Promptly cleaning off bird droppings, tree sap, and other environmental contaminants is crucial, as these can etch into the vinyl if left for extended periods. Our premium Avery Dennison and 3M materials are designed for durability, but care extends their life.

Following these simple maintenance tips will ensure your wrapped fleet continues to look professional and effectively market your business as it travels across Chicago and its surrounding suburbs for years to come.
