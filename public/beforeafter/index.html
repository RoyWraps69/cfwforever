Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I see my vehicle's 'after' transformation once I approve the design?

Once your custom design is finalized and materials are prepared, the installation process for a full wrap typically takes 2-5 business days. Partial wraps or spot graphics can often be completed within 1-3 days, allowing your vehicle to return to service as a striking 'after' advertisement very quickly.

Do the 'before' pictures show vehicles as they arrive at your Chicago facility?

Yes, our 'before' pictures are taken when the vehicle first arrives at our 4711 N Lamon Ave #7 facility. They capture the original state of the vehicle – whether it's a plain white Ford Transit or a faded commercial truck – before our expert team begins the transformation process.

What's the typical cost range for the transformations shown in the 'Before & After' gallery?

The cost for transformations varies greatly based on vehicle size and wrap coverage. Full commercial wraps for vans like those often seen in our gallery typically range from $3,800-$7,500. Partial wraps, offering significant impact, generally fall between $1,900-$3,500, showcasing a dramatic 'after' for a lower investment.

Can I request a 'before' photo of my vehicle and then see a digital 'after' proof before installation?

Absolutely! We always take 'before' photos as part of our intake process. Our design team then uses these actual vehicle images to create digital proofs, allowing you to visualize your custom 'after' wrap design directly on your specific vehicle model, ensuring complete satisfaction before production begins.

How does Chicago Fleet Wraps ensure the 'after' wrap looks as vibrant and detailed as in the gallery photos?

Our commitment to using only premium cast vinyls like Avery Dennison MPI 1105 and 3M IJ180-CV3, combined with state-of-the-art printing technology and expert installation techniques, guarantees the vibrant colors and sharp details seen in our 'after' photos. We focus on meticulous preparation and precise application.

Will the 'after' wrap protect my vehicle's original paint, as implied by some transformations?

Yes, a professionally installed wrap acts as a protective barrier for your vehicle's original paint. It shields against minor scratches, stone chips, and UV damage, helping to preserve the underlying finish. Many 'after' vehicles in our gallery look better than their 'before' state, partly due to this added protection.
