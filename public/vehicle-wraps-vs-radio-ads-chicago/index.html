When Radio Makes Sense vs Wraps

Radio is effective for top-of-mind awareness with a broad regional audience — new product launches, seasonal promotions, or companies competing for market share across the entire metro. For local service businesses with defined territories, vehicle wraps win on cost-effectiveness. The ideal marketing mix for a growing Chicago service company typically includes vehicle wraps as the always-on brand foundation, with targeted digital ads and occasional radio for seasonal pushes.
