Why we're doing this

We've wrapped Chicago vehicles for 25 years. In that time we've watched thousands of new businesses launch. Some grow into our biggest fleet accounts. Some don't make it to year two. The single biggest predictor we've seen is how visible they were in the first 90 days.

An unmarked van does no work for you when it's parked. A storefront with no signage gets walked past. The first 30 days after the city issues your license is when you should be putting your name on something visible.

So we're handing out free vinyl. Three lines of text, your choice of font and color, free install on a Saturday. Walk in with your license, walk out with your name on the truck.

Call it a welcome to the neighborhood.

Frequently asked questions

Who qualifies for free die-cut graphics?

Any business with a Chicago business license issued within the last 30 days at the time of your visit. Bring the physical or digital license to our shop at 4711 N Lamon Ave #7. We verify the issue date. One free package per licensed business.

What does the free die-cut package include?

The Grow Chicago Welcome Package is a $799 retail-value kit including: three lines of plotter-cut vinyl lettering with 10 font choices per line, sized for vehicle application on both sides plus the rear; choice of black or white premium cast vinyl with 7-year outdoor durability; free Saturday professional installation at our Portage Park shop; and gold-wrapped welcome packaging. The full $799 retail value also applies as credit toward a full vehicle wrap if you upgrade within 12 months.

Why is Chicago Fleet Wraps doing this?

Two reasons. First, community goodwill — we have wrapped Chicago vehicles for 25 years and we want new Chicago businesses to launch with proper street-level brand presence in their first 30 days. Second, lead generation — every business that participates becomes a CFW relationship that often grows into a full fleet wrap account as the business scales.

What if I do not have a vector logo file?

We can vectorize raster artwork at our standard $300 flat rate through CFW Design Labs. Note: that fee is waived if you upgrade to a full vehicle wrap with CFW within 90 days of receiving your free graphics.

Can I get color vinyl instead of black or white?

The free package is black or white only. Color vinyl, full-color digital print, metallic finishes, or specialty materials are available at standard CFW pricing. Black and white are the highest-contrast options for vehicle and storefront applications and read at the longest distances.

Can Chicago Fleet Wraps install the graphics for me?

Yes — and Saturday installation is FREE as part of the Grow Chicago program. Bring your vehicle to our Portage Park shop on Saturday and we install the graphics on both sides and rear at no charge. Weekday installs are available at standard rates ($150+) for businesses that cannot make a Saturday.

How long does the cut take?

Three business days from artwork approval. Same-day or rush turnaround is available at standard rush rates if you are launching for an event or grand opening.

Where do I bring my license?

Chicago Fleet Wraps, 4711 N Lamon Ave #7, Chicago, IL 60630 — Portage Park, three blocks off the Kennedy Expressway. Walk-ins welcome during business hours, or fill out the form on this page and we will follow up to schedule your visit.

Does this promotion expire?

No fixed expiration. The Grow Chicago promotion runs as an ongoing welcome program for newly licensed Chicago businesses. Every business that walks in within 30 days of their license issue date qualifies, regardless of when you read this page.

Can I use this offer if my business was licensed more than 30 days ago?

No — the 30-day window is firm. The promotion is specifically designed to support businesses in their critical first month. If your license is older than 30 days, you can still get die-cut graphics from CFW at standard pricing, which starts at $180 for a basic decal set.
