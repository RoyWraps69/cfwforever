Why Plumbers Wrap Their Vans in Chicago

Plumbing vans park in driveways and alleys across Chicago's neighborhoods for 1–4 hours per job. In dense areas like Wicker Park, Roscoe Village, and Evanston, that's hundreds of potential customers seeing your van daily. Wrapped vans generate inbound calls from neighbors who didn't know they needed a plumber until they saw your truck outside.

What to Put on a Plumber Van Wrap

  • Company name and logo — top of both sides, readable at 50 feet
  • Phone number — 4-inch digits, both sides
  • Services — emergency, drain cleaning, water heaters, repiping
  • "24/7 Emergency" callout — drives after-hours calls at premium rates
  • License number — Illinois plumbing license builds trust
  • Website and QR code — drives online booking

Illinois Plumbing License on Van Wraps

Illinois requires licensed plumbers to display their plumbing contractor license number on commercial vehicles. Chicago Fleet Wraps integrates your license number and required disclosures into the wrap design at no additional charge.
