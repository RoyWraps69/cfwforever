What Our Warranty Covers (and What It Doesn't)

Our 2-year workmanship warranty covers defects directly related to our installation, such as lifting, tunneling, or bubbling caused by improper application. The manufacturer's warranty covers material defects like premature fading, cracking, or delamination of the vinyl itself under normal environmental conditions. Together, these provide comprehensive coverage for your fleet's visual branding.

It's important to understand that our warranty does not cover damage resulting from accidents, acts of vandalism, improper cleaning methods (e.g., pressure washing too close), or normal wear and tear over time. Similarly, pre-existing paint damage or rust on the vehicle prior to installation is not covered. We always recommend a clean, factory-finish surface for optimal wrap longevity and warranty validity.

Frequently Asked Questions

What specifically does the 2-year workmanship warranty from Chicago Fleet Wraps cover?

Our 2-year workmanship warranty covers any issues directly attributable to our installation process, such as lifting, bubbling, or peeling of the wrap caused by improper application. This ensures your investment is protected against installation-related defects for two full years after your fleet wrap project is completed.

How long do the manufacturer warranties typically last for Avery Dennison and 3M materials?

Manufacturer warranties for Avery Dennison MPI 1105 and 3M IJ180-CV3 cast vinyl typically range from 5 to 7 years for vertical surfaces, depending on the specific product and color. These warranties cover material defects like premature fading, cracking, or delamination under normal conditions.

Does the warranty cover damage from power washing or automatic car washes?

No, our warranty does not cover damage caused by improper cleaning methods, including aggressive power washing or certain automatic car washes. We recommend hand washing with mild soap and water to maintain the integrity and longevity of your vehicle wrap, preserving both our workmanship and the material warranty.

What happens if I have a warranty claim? What's the process?

If you believe you have a warranty claim, please contact Chicago Fleet Wraps directly at (312) 971-3828. We will schedule an inspection of the vehicle at our 4711 N Lawndale Ave, Chicago IL 60625 facility to assess the issue and determine if it falls under our workmanship or the manufacturer's material warranty. We aim for prompt resolution.

Is paint damage covered if the wrap is removed by Chicago Fleet Wraps?

While our track record shows zero verified paint damage claims, our warranty does not explicitly cover pre-existing paint issues. However, if our certified technicians perform the removal and damage occurs due to our process on a vehicle with original, healthy paint, we address it. Our use of premium materials minimizes this risk significantly.

Does the warranty cover partial wraps or just full wraps?

Our 2-year workmanship warranty applies to all wrap installations performed by Chicago Fleet Wraps, whether it's a full wrap, partial wrap, or even lettering and decals. The quality of our application and the integrity of the materials are guaranteed across all our services, ensuring consistent protection for your Chicago fleet.

What information do I need to provide if I need to make a warranty claim?

To expedite a warranty claim, please provide your company name, the vehicle's make, model, and license plate number, the date of installation (if known), and a detailed description of the issue, ideally with photographs. This information helps us quickly locate your project details and assess the situation at our Chicago facility.
