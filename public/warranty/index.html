Wrap Warranty

Every fleet wrap from Chicago Fleet Wraps includes a 2-year workmanship warranty plus manufacturer material warranties from Avery Dennison and 3M.

Workmanship Warranty — 2 Years

  • Covers lifting, peeling, bubbling, or edge failure due to installation
  • Free repair or replacement of affected panels
  • Applies to all fleet wraps installed by our team

Material Warranty — 5–7 Years

  • Avery Dennison MPI 1105: 7-year outdoor vertical durability
  • 3M IJ180-CV3: 5-year outdoor vertical durability
  • DOL 1360 overlaminate: UV and scratch protection rated to match film life
  • Covers fading, cracking, and delamination under normal conditions

What's Not Covered

  • Damage from collisions, accidents, or vandalism
  • Brush car wash or high-pressure washer damage
  • Chemical exposure (solvents, fuel spills, harsh degreasers)
  • Normal wear on high-contact areas (door handles, step rails)
