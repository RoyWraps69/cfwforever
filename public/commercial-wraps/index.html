Essential Maintenance Tips for Your Chicago Commercial Vehicle Wraps

To ensure your commercial vehicle wraps maintain their vibrant appearance and longevity in the demanding Chicago environment, proper maintenance is key. We recommend hand washing your wrapped vehicles regularly with a mild, non-abrasive detergent and soft cloth. Avoid automated car washes that use harsh brushes or high-pressure sprays, as these can damage the vinyl and compromise adhesion.

Promptly remove any spills, bird droppings, or tree sap to prevent staining or etching of the wrap material. While our premium Avery Dennison and 3M films are highly durable, consistent care, especially after driving through slushy winter streets or dusty construction sites, will preserve their pristine condition. Following these simple guidelines will ensure your brand continues to shine brightly across Chicagoland for years to come.
