Evanston Fleet Wrap FAQ

How close is your shop to Evanston?

Our shop at 4711 N. Lamon Ave in Chicago is just 12 miles from downtown Evanston — about a 20-minute drive via the Edens Expressway (I-94). We also offer free pickup and delivery if you prefer not to drive your vehicles to us.

Do you wrap vehicles for Northwestern University vendors?

What vinyl quality is best for Evanston's lakefront climate?
