Materials We Use — Only Premium Cast Vinyl

We exclusively use premium cast vinyl from the two industry leaders. This is what separates the best vehicle wraps from cheap wraps that crack, peel, and fade within a year:

  • Avery Dennison MPI 1105 — Premium cast vinyl with Supreme Wrapping Film overlaminate. Rated for 7+ years outdoor durability. Our preferred film for fleet and commercial wraps.

We never use calendered (cheap) vinyl. Cast vinyl conforms to curves without shrinking, cracking, or peeling — critical for Chicago's extreme temperature swings from -10°F winters to 95°F+ summers. If another shop is quoting you 40% less, ask what vinyl they use.
