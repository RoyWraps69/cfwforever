Commercial vs. Personal Vehicle Wraps

Commercial vehicle wraps are designed to generate leads and build brand recognition. Every wrapped commercial vehicle generates 30,000–70,000+ daily impressions at a cost of just $0.04–$0.07 per thousand — making fleet wraps the lowest-cost advertising medium available. Commercial vehicle wraps →

Personal vehicle wraps let you customize your ride with unique colors, finishes, and graphics without permanent paint changes. Wraps protect your factory paint and can be removed without damage, preserving resale value. Popular for Teslas, BMWs, sports cars, and luxury SUVs.

Materials We Use

We exclusively use premium cast vinyl from the two industry leaders:

  • Avery Dennison MPI 1105 — Premium cast vinyl with Supreme Wrapping Film overlaminate. Rated for 7+ years outdoor durability.
  • 3M IJ180-CV3 — Controltac cast vinyl with 3M 8518 overlaminate. Rated for 5–7 years outdoor durability.
  • Specialty Films — Chrome, color-shift, carbon fiber, brushed metal, and textured finishes from Avery, 3M, and Inozetek.

We never use calendered (cheap) vinyl. Cast vinyl conforms to curves without shrinking, cracking, or peeling — critical for Chicago's extreme temperature swings from -10°F winters to 95°F+ summers.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do vehicle wraps cost in Chicago?

Vehicle wrap costs range from $1,500 for partial wraps to $10,000+ for large commercial vehicles. Sedans cost $2,500–$4,000, SUVs $3,000–$5,000, cargo vans $2,800–$4,500, and box trucks $4,200–$10,000. See full pricing →

How long do vehicle wraps last?

Premium cast vinyl wraps from Avery Dennison and 3M last 5–7 years with proper care. Our wraps include a 2-year workmanship warranty covering lifting, peeling, and bubbling.

Will a vehicle wrap damage my paint?

No. Premium cast vinyl wraps actually protect your paint from UV damage, scratches, and road debris. When professionally removed, the original paint is preserved in pristine condition.

How long does vehicle wrap installation take?

Most vehicle wraps take 2–5 business days depending on vehicle size and design complexity. We offer free pickup and delivery across Chicagoland so your downtime is minimized.
