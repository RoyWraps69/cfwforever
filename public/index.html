How much does a fleet wrap cost in Chicago?

Fleet wrap pricing in Chicago is based on vehicle square footage and vinyl tier. Premium cast vinyl (Avery Dennison MPI 1105 / 3M IJ180-CV3) is $9.75/sqft and color change wraps are $8.25/sqft. Cargo van full wraps start at $3,750, Sprinter van wraps from $4,700, and box truck wraps range from $5,000–$10,900 depending on length (12–26 ft). All prices include custom design, premium cast vinyl, professional installation, and free pickup throughout Chicagoland. Fleet discounts: 3% for 2–4 vehicles, 7% for 5–9, 11% for 10–24, and 15% for 25+. See full pricing details →

Are vehicle wraps worth it for small businesses?

Yes. A single wrapped vehicle generates 30,000–70,000 daily impressions at a cost of just $0.04–$0.07 per 1,000 impressions — compared to $6–$12 for Google Ads. Vehicle wraps are also 100% tax deductible under IRS Section 179. With a 5–7 year lifespan, a fleet wrap delivers 2,500–5,000% ROI over its lifetime, making it the most cost-effective advertising for local service businesses.

How long do commercial vehicle wraps last?

Commercial vehicle wraps using premium cast vinyl (Avery Dennison MPI 1105 or 3M IJ180-CV3) are rated for 5–7 years of outdoor use. With proper care — hand washing, avoiding automated brush car washes, and keeping pressure washers below 1,200 PSI — fleet wraps in Chicago regularly last 6–7 years looking sharp despite harsh winters, road salt, and UV exposure.

What is the best material for commercial vehicle wraps?

Cast vinyl is the industry standard for commercial fleet wraps. Chicago Fleet Wraps exclusively uses Avery Dennison MPI 1105 and 3M IJ180-CV3 — both premium cast vinyl films rated for 5–7 years outdoor. Cast vinyl conforms to curves and rivets without lifting, unlike cheaper calendered vinyl which shrinks and fails within 1–2 years. Chicago Fleet Wraps guarantees proper application with a 2-year workmanship warranty.
