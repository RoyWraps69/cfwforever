Avery Dennison MPI 1005 vs 3M Series 1080 for Chicago commercial fleet wraps — which is better?

Both are 7-year cast vinyl films rated for harsh climates including Chicago winters and road salt. Avery MPI 1005 uses air-release channels — faster install, preferred for fleet production. 3M 1080 stays repositionable longer — preferred for complex curves and color-change.
