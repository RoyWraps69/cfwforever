Vehicle Wrap Removal

Professional wrap removal for fleet vehicles, color change wraps, and commercial graphics in Chicago. We remove wraps cleanly without damaging the original paint — guaranteed.

Our Removal Services

  • Full wrap removal — fleet graphics, color change, and advertising wraps
  • Partial removal — decals, lettering, and spot graphics
  • Adhesive cleanup — heat gun and chemical-free residue removal
  • Re-wrap prep — surface preparation for new wrap installation
  • Fleet removal — bulk pricing for multiple vehicles

How We Remove Wraps

Our certified technicians use controlled heat to soften the adhesive and peel the vinyl at the correct angle. This preserves the factory paint underneath. After removal, we clean all adhesive residue so the surface is ready for a new wrap or bare paint.

Pricing

Wrap removal pricing depends on vehicle size, wrap age, and vinyl type. Older wraps and calendered vinyl take longer to remove. Contact us for a free removal quote — we'll give you real pricing, not a range.
