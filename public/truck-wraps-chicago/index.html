Full Wraps vs. Partial Truck Wraps

Full truck wraps cover every visible panel for maximum impact. On a box truck, that means 200+ square feet of billboard-quality advertising space per side. Full wraps generate the most impressions and deliver the best ROI for high-visibility routes.

Partial truck wraps cover strategic panels — sides, tailgate, rear doors — at 40–60% of full wrap cost. Partial wraps are ideal for businesses starting their fleet branding or working with tighter budgets. Learn more about partial wraps →

Frequently Asked Questions About Truck Wraps

How much do truck wraps cost in Chicago?

Truck wrap cost depends on vehicle size: pickup truck wraps cost $2,200–$3,800, box truck wraps (16–22 ft) cost $4,200–$6,700, and large box truck wraps (24–26 ft) cost $7,000–$10,000. Partial truck wraps start at $1,200. Fleet discounts of 5–15% apply for 3+ trucks.

What types of trucks can you wrap?

We wrap all truck types: pickup trucks (F-150, Silverado, Ram, Tundra), box trucks (16–26 ft), flatbed trucks, stake trucks, dump trucks, cement mixers, tow trucks, and specialty commercial vehicles. If it has panels, we can wrap it.

Full truck wrap vs partial truck wrap — which is better?

Full truck wraps provide maximum visual impact and the best ROI for high-traffic routes. Partial truck wraps cover key panels at 40–60% of the cost and are ideal for businesses starting their truck branding. For commercial trucks on Chicago expressways, full wraps generate significantly more impressions.

How long does a truck wrap last?

Our truck wraps use premium Avery Dennison MPI 1105 and 3M IJ180-CV3 cast vinyl rated for 5–7 years outdoors. Combined with our 2-year installation warranty, your truck wrap is built for years of commercial use in Chicago's extreme weather.

Will a truck wrap damage my truck's paint?

No. Chicago Fleet Wraps has zero verified paint damage claims in over 10 years. Cast vinyl truck wraps actually protect your factory paint from UV rays, minor scratches, and road debris. When removed, your original paint is preserved.

How long does truck wrap installation take?

Pickup truck wraps take 1–2 days. Box truck wraps (16–22 ft) take 2–3 days. Large box truck wraps (24–26 ft) take 3–4 days. We offer free pickup and delivery across Chicagoland so your truck downtime is minimized.

Can I get a custom truck wrap design?

Yes — every truck wrap starts with a free custom design mockup on your exact vehicle template. Our in-house design team creates truck wrap designs that maximize visual impact. Revisions are included until you're 100% satisfied.

Are truck wraps tax deductible?

Yes — commercial truck wraps are 100% tax deductible as a business advertising expense under IRS Section 179. The full cost of your truck wrap can be deducted in the year of installation.

Do you wrap pickup trucks?

Yes — we wrap all pickup trucks including Ford F-150/F-250/F-350, Chevy Silverado 1500/2500/3500, Ram 1500/2500/3500, Toyota Tundra, and GMC Sierra. Pickup truck vinyl wraps start at $2,200 for full coverage.

Can truck wraps be removed?

Yes. Truck wraps can be professionally removed without damaging paint. If you rebrand, sell the truck, or update your design, we can remove and replace truck wraps. Truck wrap removal typically takes 4–8 hours.
