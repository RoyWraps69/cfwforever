Full Wraps vs. Partial Truck Wraps

Full truck wraps cover every visible panel for maximum impact. On a box truck, that means 200+ square feet of billboard-quality advertising space per side. Full wraps generate the most impressions and deliver the best ROI for high-visibility routes.

Partial truck wraps cover strategic panels — sides, tailgate, rear doors — at 40–60% of full wrap cost. Partial wraps are ideal for businesses starting their fleet branding or working with tighter budgets. Learn more about partial wraps →

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do truck wraps cost in Chicago?

Pickup truck wraps cost $2,200–$3,800. Box trucks range from $4,200–$10,000 depending on size. Partial wraps start at $1,200. Fleet discounts of 5–15% apply for 3+ trucks.

What types of trucks can you wrap?

We wrap all truck types: pickup trucks, box trucks (16–26 ft), flatbed trucks, stake trucks, dump trucks, cement mixers, and specialty commercial vehicles.

Full wrap vs partial wrap — which is better?

Full wraps provide maximum visual impact and advertising space. Partial wraps cover key panels at 40–60% of the cost. For commercial trucks on high-traffic routes, full wraps generate significantly more impressions and ROI.

How long does a truck wrap last?

Our truck wraps use premium cast vinyl rated for 5–7 years outdoors. Combined with our 2-year installation warranty, your wrap is built for years of commercial use in Chicago weather.
