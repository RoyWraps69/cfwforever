Frequently Asked Questions

What is the ROI on a fleet wrap?

A single wrapped cargo van generates 30,000–70,000 daily impressions at a cost-per-thousand of $0.04–$0.07 over a 5-year lifespan — compared to $15–$25 CPM for Google Ads. Chicago Fleet Wraps clients report the median fleet wrap pays for itself in increased inbound calls within 12–18 months.

How do I track leads from my vehicle wrap?

Use a dedicated phone number on your wrap that forwards to your main line — this lets you track exactly how many calls came from the wrap. QR codes on the rear of the vehicle also work for measuring digital engagement from wrap viewers.

Is a fleet wrap better than Google Ads for local businesses?

They serve different purposes. Google Ads captures intent — people already searching for your service. Vehicle wraps create awareness before the need exists. The most effective Chicago service businesses use wraps for brand presence and Google Ads for intent capture. The wrap CPM is dramatically lower.

How many impressions does a wrapped van get in Chicago?

According to OAAA data, a single wrapped vehicle in a Chicago metro route generates 30,000–70,000 impressions per day. Vans operating on the Kennedy, Dan Ryan, or Eisenhower expressways or in dense neighborhoods like Lincoln Park, Wicker Park, and the Loop report the highest numbers.

What percentage of fleet wrap clients see measurable ROI?

In a Chicago Fleet Wraps client survey, 34% of clients actively track wrap-attributed leads using a dedicated phone number or QR code. Of those tracking, the median is 12 inbound calls per month directly attributed to their wrapped vehicles.
