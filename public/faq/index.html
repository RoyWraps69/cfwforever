Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about fleet wraps — pricing, materials, timeline, and process.

How much does a fleet wrap cost?

Cargo van full wraps start at $2,800–$4,500. Box trucks range from $4,200–$10,000 depending on size. Fleet discounts available for 3+ vehicles. Use our Wrap Calculator for instant pricing.

How long does a vehicle wrap last?

Premium cast vinyl (Avery Dennison MPI 1105 / 3M IJ180-CV3) lasts 5–7 years outdoors with proper care. See our Wrap Care Guide.

Are vehicle wraps tax deductible?

Yes. Commercial vehicle wraps are 100% deductible under IRS Section 179 as a business advertising expense. Consult your tax professional for details.

How long does installation take?

Cargo vans: 1–2 business days. Sprinter vans: 2–3 days. Box trucks: 2–4 days depending on size. Fleet orders are phased to minimize downtime.

Do you pick up our vehicles?

Yes. Free fleet pickup and delivery anywhere in Chicagoland — Cook, DuPage, Kane, Lake, Will, and McHenry counties.

Will a wrap damage my vehicle's paint?

No. Premium cast vinyl is designed for clean removal. Wraps actually protect the original paint from UV damage and minor scratches.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the entire Chicagoland area including Chicago, Schaumburg, Naperville, Aurora, Elgin, Joliet, Evanston, and 75+ cities. See our full service area.
