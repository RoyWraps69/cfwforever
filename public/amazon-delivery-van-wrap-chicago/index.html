After the Wrap — Care, Warranty, and Reorders

Every Rivian EDV wrap installation comes with a robust 2-year workmanship warranty, which specifically covers issues like lifting, bubbling, edge peeling, and any installation-related defects. This guarantee is distinct from the vinyl manufacturer's warranty; both Avery Dennison and 3M provide their own film performance warranties, typically ranging from 5–7 years for outdoor use on commercial vehicles like these Amazon delivery vans.

To maximize the longevity of your Amazon Rivian EDV wrap, always hand wash it using mild soap and water. Avoid automated brush car washes, as they can scratch the vinyl and lift edges. When using pressure washers, keep them below 1,200 PSI and at least 12 inches away from panel edges. Promptly address any bird droppings or tree sap, as these acidic compounds can etch into the vinyl if left on the surface for extended periods.

Should you expand your Amazon delivery fleet with additional Rivian EDVs in the future, your specific brand files are securely and permanently stored with us. We never discard fleet artwork, ensuring consistency for your branding. This means reorders for new EDV wraps move into production much faster, as the initial design approval process is already complete; we simply adapt your existing Amazon brand standards to the new Rivian EDV template and submit it for a quick final review.
