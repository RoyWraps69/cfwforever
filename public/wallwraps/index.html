Materials

We use 3M and Avery Dennison wall-rated vinyl specifically designed for interior and exterior architectural surfaces:

  • 3M IJ180mC-10 — cast vinyl with high-tack adhesive for textured drywall and concrete
  • Avery Dennison MPI 2611 — wall vinyl rated for smooth and lightly textured surfaces
  • 3M Envision 48C — PVC-free wall vinyl for LEED-certified and eco-conscious spaces
  • UV-resistant overlaminate — standard on exterior installations for 5–7 year durability
  • Anti-graffiti laminate — optional protection for public-facing exterior wraps

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do wall wraps cost?

Logo walls start at $500. Full wall murals range from $1,200–$5,000. Large commercial installations can run $5,000–$20,000+. Pricing depends on wall size, surface, and design complexity.

Can you wrap textured walls?

Yes. We use high-tack adhesive vinyl designed for textured surfaces including drywall, concrete block, and stucco.

How long do wall wraps last?

Interior: 7–10+ years. Exterior: 5–7 years with UV overlaminate. Indoor wraps see minimal wear and often last the life of the space.

Can wall wraps be removed?

Yes. Quality wall vinyl is removable. Wall prep quality affects removal — well-primed walls see cleaner removal.

Do you install on-site?

Yes. All wall wrap installations are done on-site at your location. We serve all of Chicagoland.
