What Are Wall Wraps and How Much Do They Cost?

Wall wraps are large-format vinyl graphics applied to interior or exterior walls for branding, wayfinding, or decoration. Pricing ranges from $8–$15 per square foot installed. A standard 8x20-foot wall wrap costs $1,200–$2,400 including design, material, and professional installation.

  • Interior office wall graphics — mission statements, brand values, culture walls, and employee recognition
  • Lobby and reception murals — first-impression branding for clients and visitors
  • Retail storefront graphics — window wraps, promotional displays, and seasonal graphics
  • Exterior building wraps — large-format building graphics and directional signage
  • Wayfinding and directional — floor graphics, directional arrows, and room identification
  • Trade show and event graphics — temporary wall wraps for conventions and pop-up events
  • Restaurant and bar graphics — custom murals, menu boards, and atmosphere graphics
  • Gym and fitness center wraps — motivational graphics and branding for workout spaces

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of surfaces can Chicago Fleet Wraps apply wall wraps to?

We can apply wall wraps to a wide variety of smooth, clean, and painted surfaces, including drywall, plaster, glass, and some metals. For textured surfaces like brick or concrete, specialized conformable films are available, which we can discuss during your consultation to ensure optimal adhesion and finish for your Chicago location.

How long do wall wraps from Chicago Fleet Wraps typically last?

With our premium Avery Dennison MPI 1105 and 3M IJ180-CV3 cast vinyls, interior wall wraps can last 5-10 years or more, depending on environmental factors and care. Exterior wall wraps, exposed to Chicago's weather, typically have a lifespan of 3-7 years, maintaining their vibrant appearance and structural integrity.

Can I remove a wall wrap without damaging the underlying paint or surface?

Yes, when professionally installed and removed by experienced technicians like ours, our premium cast vinyl wall wraps are designed to be removed cleanly without damaging the underlying painted surface. This makes them an excellent temporary or long-term branding solution for your Chicago business.

Do wall wraps require special cleaning or maintenance in a Chicago commercial setting?

Wall wraps are remarkably low-maintenance. For most applications, simply wiping them down with a soft cloth and mild, non-abrasive cleaner (like dish soap and water) is sufficient to keep them looking fresh. Avoid harsh chemicals or abrasive scrubbing to preserve the print and laminate quality.

Can Chicago Fleet Wraps help with the design of my wall wrap for my business?

Absolutely! Our in-house design team specializes in creating impactful and brand-aligned wall wrap designs. Whether you have a concept, existing brand assets, or need full creative direction, we work closely with your Chicago business to bring your vision to life, ensuring it resonates with your target audience.

Is there a minimum or maximum size for wall wrap projects in Chicago?

There is no strict minimum or maximum size. We handle projects ranging from small accent walls or door graphics to entire building facades or multi-room installations. We cater to all scales of commercial wall wrap needs across Chicago and the surrounding suburbs, providing custom solutions for every space.

What's the difference between a full wall wrap and a partial wall graphic?

A full wall wrap covers an entire wall surface from edge to edge, creating a seamless mural effect. A partial wall graphic covers only a section of the wall, often incorporating the existing wall color as part of the design, or featuring specific logos, lettering, or images. Both are effective for Chicago businesses.
