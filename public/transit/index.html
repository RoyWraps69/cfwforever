Maintenance and Longevity of Your Transit Van Wrap

Maintaining the pristine appearance of your Ford Transit van wrap is straightforward and contributes significantly to its longevity. We recommend hand washing your wrapped vans using mild soap and water. Avoid abrasive brushes, high-pressure washers directed too closely, and harsh chemical cleaners, as these can damage the vinyl film and shorten its lifespan. Regular cleaning helps preserve the vibrant graphics.

With proper care, a premium Transit van wrap from Chicago Fleet Wraps, utilizing Avery Dennison MPI 1105 or 3M IJ180-CV3, can easily last 5 to 7 years. Frequently Asked Questions

Can a Transit van wrap cover the windows?

Yes, we can apply perforated window film to the rear and side windows of your Transit van. This specialized material allows passengers or drivers to see out from the inside while displaying your graphics clearly on the exterior, maintaining privacy and advertising space.

How long does a Transit van wrap typically last in Chicago's climate?

With our premium Avery Dennison MPI 1105 or 3M IJ180-CV3 cast vinyl and professional installation, your Transit van wrap is designed to last 5 to 7 years, even through Chicago's varied weather conditions, from snowy winters to hot, humid summers.

Will a Transit van wrap damage the original paint of my Ford Transit?

Absolutely not. Our premium cast vinyl films are designed with advanced adhesive technology that protects your van's original paint. When professionally removed, they leave no residue or damage, preserving your vehicle's resale value. We have zero verified paint damage claims since 2001.

Do I need to provide my own design for my Transit van wrap?

While you're welcome to provide your own design, our in-house design team at Chicago Fleet Wraps specializes in creating impactful graphics tailored for Transit vans. We'll work with you to develop a compelling design that effectively promotes your brand across Chicago.

What's the difference between a full wrap and a partial wrap for a Transit van?

A full wrap covers virtually the entire exterior of your Transit van, offering maximum advertising space. A partial wrap covers a significant portion, often combining vinyl graphics with the van's original paint color, providing a cost-effective branding solution while still making a strong visual statement.

Can Chicago Fleet Wraps handle wrapping a large fleet of Transit vans?

Yes, absolutely. We are fully equipped and experienced in managing large fleet wrap projects, including multiple Ford Transit vans. Our efficient process and dedicated team ensure consistent branding and timely completion for your entire Chicago-area fleet.

Is free fleet pickup available for all Transit van wrap services?

Yes, Chicago Fleet Wraps proudly offers complimentary fleet pickup and delivery services across the entire Chicagoland area for all our vehicle wrap services, including Transit van wraps. This convenience helps minimize downtime for your business operations.
