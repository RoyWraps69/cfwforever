Caring for Your Wrapped Sprinter: Maintenance Tips

To ensure your Sprinter van wrap maintains its pristine appearance and maximizes its lifespan, proper care is essential. We recommend hand washing your wrapped vehicle with mild, non-abrasive detergents and a soft cloth or sponge. Avoid automatic car washes, especially those with brushes, as these can scratch or damage the vinyl and lift edges over time. High-pressure washing should be used with caution, keeping the nozzle at least 12 inches away from the surface and avoiding direct blasts at seams or edges.

Regular cleaning, particularly after exposure to harsh elements like salt during Chicago winters or bird droppings, will prevent staining and degradation of the vinyl. Promptly clean any fuel spills to prevent damage to the laminate. With proper care, your premium Sprinter wrap from CFW will continue to look vibrant and professional for 5-7 years, keeping your brand looking sharp as you travel from Schaumburg to South Shore.

Sprinter Van Wrap Specifications — Installer's Data

Material required for a full Sprinter wrap: 144" standard wheelbase requires approximately 275–300 sq ft of printed vinyl. The 170" extended wheelbase requires 325–350 sq ft. High-roof models add 40–50 sq ft for the additional roof panel height.

Installation time: A full Sprinter wrap takes 16–22 labor hours depending on design complexity. Simple one-color designs with minimal contour cuts take 16 hours. Complex multi-panel designs with window perf and rear door wraps take 20–22 hours.

Sprinter-specific challenges: The corrugated side panels on 2019+ models require extra heat and post-heating to prevent lifting. The sliding door track area needs precise trimming to maintain door function. We use knifeless tape on all Sprinter sliding door seams to prevent blade damage to the paint.

Pricing breakdown: Full wrap on a standard 144" Sprinter: $3,800–$4,800. Extended 170": $4,200–$5,500. High-roof 170" EXT: $4,800–$6,200. Partial wrap (sides and rear only): $2,200–$3,400. These prices include design, print on 3M IJ180Cv3, 8519 laminate, and installation with a 2-year labor warranty.

Most popular Sprinter wrap designs: 62% of our Sprinter clients choose a full wrap. 24% choose partial (sides + rear). 14% choose lettering and logo only. The most common industries ordering Sprinter wraps are delivery services (28%), HVAC/plumbing (22%), and catering/food service (18%).
