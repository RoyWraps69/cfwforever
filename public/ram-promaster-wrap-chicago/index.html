After the Wrap — Care, Warranty, and Reorders

Each Ram ProMaster wrap comes with a 2-year workmanship warranty that covers issues like lifting, bubbling, edge peeling, and installation defects. This is separate from the manufacturer's vinyl warranty; both Avery Dennison and 3M provide their own film performance warranties of 5–7 years for outdoor use on your ProMaster.

To maximize the lifespan of your Ram ProMaster wrap, we recommend hand washing with mild soap and water. Avoid automated brush car washes, as they can scratch the vinyl and lift edges. Keep pressure washers below 1,200 PSI and at least 12 inches away from panel edges. Promptly address bird droppings and tree sap, as these acidic compounds can etch into the vinyl if left uncleaned on your ProMaster.

When you expand your Ram ProMaster fleet by adding vehicles months or even years down the line, your branding files are permanently archived with us. We never discard fleet artwork. This means reorders for new ProMaster wraps go into production much faster, as design approval is already complete – we simply adapt your existing brand standards to the new Ram ProMaster template and submit it for a quick review.

Understanding the Ram ProMaster's Unique Wrapping Challenges

The Ram ProMaster, with its distinct high roof and boxy profile, presents specific considerations for a perfect wrap. Its relatively flat and expansive side panels are ideal for large graphics, but the corrugation on the lower body and the integrated rear door hinges require specialized techniques to ensure proper adhesion and a smooth finish. Our expert installers are highly experienced in navigating these nuances, preventing common issues like lifting or bubbling.

A typical full wrap for a Ram ProMaster involves covering approximately 250-300 square feet of surface area, depending on the wheelbase and roof height. The vehicle's design, often featuring large, uninterrupted panels, allows for striking visual statements, but also demands precise alignment and wrinkle-free application across these vast surfaces. Our team meticulously plans each panel placement to ensure design continuity and a professional appearance from every angle.
