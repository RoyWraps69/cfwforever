What Billboards Do That Wraps Cannot

Billboards do have one genuine advantage: scale. A 14x48 ft billboard visible from I-90 reaches a massive simultaneous audience in a fixed location. For brand awareness campaigns targeting highway commuters, billboards have a place. But for a local service business — HVAC, plumbing, contracting, delivery — a wrap that drives through Lincoln Park, Wicker Park, and Logan Square in a single day reaches the exact homeowners who need you.
