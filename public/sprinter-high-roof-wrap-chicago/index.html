Frequently Asked Questions — Sprinter High Roof / Super High Roof Wraps Chicago

How much does a high-roof Sprinter wrap cost in Chicago?

A Mercedes Sprinter high-roof full wrap in Chicago starts at $5,200 for the 144" wheelbase. The 170" and 170" extended models run $5,600–$5,900. Super high-roof variants add another $400–$600. The additional cost over standard-roof Sprinters reflects the extra surface area, the height of installation, and the more complex upper panel work.

Why does the high-roof Sprinter cost more to wrap than the standard roof?

Three reasons: more material (roughly 80–100 additional square feet), more installation time (working at height takes longer and requires greater care), and more complex upper panel transitions. The investment is worth it — a fully wrapped high-roof Sprinter has one of the best cost-per-impression ratios of any vehicle in the commercial fleet.

Is the Sprinter roof typically included in the wrap?

We offer two options: a full wrap including the roof, or a wrap that terminates at the roofline. The roof is typically included when the vehicle parks in areas where it can be seen from above — roof decks, elevated highways, parking garages. For vehicles that never park in elevated-view locations, leaving the roof unwrapped saves money without sacrificing visibility.

Can you match a high-roof Sprinter wrap to our standard-roof Sprinter fleet?

Yes — we maintain brand consistency across all Sprinter configurations. Your 144" standard roof, 170" high roof, and any other Sprinter variants will all carry the same brand identity with Pantone-matched colors and consistent graphic placement adapted to each model's dimensions.

After the Wrap — Care, Warranty, and Reorders

Every Sprinter High Roof or Super High Roof wrap includes a 2-year workmanship warranty, which covers issues like lifting, bubbling, edge peeling, and installation flaws. This warranty is distinct from the manufacturer's vinyl warranty; both Avery Dennison and 3M offer their own film performance guarantees of 5–7 years for outdoor use on commercial vehicles.

To extend the life of your Sprinter High Roof or Super High Roof wrap, hand wash it using mild soap and water, and avoid automated brush car washes which can scratch the vinyl and lift edges. When using pressure washers, keep them below 1,200 PSI and at least 12 inches from the panel edges. Promptly address bird droppings and tree sap, as these acidic substances can etch into the vinyl if left on your high-roof van.

When you expand your Sprinter fleet with additional high-roof vans in the future, your brand files are permanently archived with us. We never delete fleet artwork.
