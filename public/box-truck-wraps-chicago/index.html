Protecting Your Investment: Maintenance & Longevity for Your Box Truck Wrap

Maintaining the pristine condition and longevity of your box truck wrap is simpler than you might think. Regular hand washing with a mild detergent and water is recommended, avoiding abrasive brushes or high-pressure washes that can damage the vinyl. Pay particular attention to edges and seams to prevent lifting, especially after navigating through Chicago's demanding weather conditions.

We advise against automatic car washes with rotating brushes, as these can scratch the vinyl and compromise its finish. With proper care, your premium Avery Dennison or 3M box truck wrap will maintain its vibrant appearance and protective qualities for 5-7 years, often exceeding the lifespan of paint in terms of color retention and resistance to minor abrasions from daily operations in areas like Logan Square or Englewood.
