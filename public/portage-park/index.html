Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum fleet size to get a discount?

Fleet discounts start at 2 vehicles (3% discount). The discount increases with fleet size: 7% for 5–9 vehicles, 11% for 10–24, and 15% for 25 or more. There is no minimum fleet size requirement — we wrap single vehicles for solo operators every day.

How do you handle the design for a first-time wrap customer?

We create a custom design mockup on your exact vehicle template. If you have existing brand assets (logo, colors, fonts), we work from those. If you need branding created from scratch, we can help with that too. You review the mockup, request changes, and approve before we print anything. Unlimited revisions are included.

Can a wrap be removed if I sell the vehicle?

Yes — cast vinyl wraps are fully removable. Professional removal by a trained technician leaves the factory paint intact and typically in better condition than unwrapped vehicles of the same age, since the vinyl protects the paint from UV and minor abrasion. We offer wrap removal as a separate service.

What is the turnaround time from estimate to wrapped vehicle?

Typical turnaround from first contact to wrapped vehicle is 5–10 business days: 1–2 days for estimate and design kickoff, 3–5 days for design approval and scheduling, 1–2 days for installation. Rush jobs are available with advance notice — call (312) 597-1286 to discuss your timeline.
