Frequently Asked Questions — Ram ProMaster City Wraps Chicago

How much does a Ram ProMaster City wrap cost?

A Ram ProMaster City full wrap in Chicago starts at $2,200. Partial wraps covering both sides and the tailgate start at $1,200. The ProMaster City's European-style profile with its prominent wheel arches can make for a distinctive-looking wrap when designed to incorporate rather than ignore those features.

How does the ProMaster City compare to the Transit Connect for wrapping?

Both compact vans wrap at similar price points. The ProMaster City has more pronounced wheel arches and a single tailgate that creates a larger rear canvas. The Transit Connect has split rear barn doors on most configurations. The choice between them for wrapping purposes usually comes down to whichever van the client already owns.

Is the ProMaster City still being made?

Ram discontinued the ProMaster City after 2022. However, many remain in active commercial service, and they wrap and perform well. If you have a ProMaster City with years of service life remaining, a professional wrap is worth every dollar.

Can you wrap the windows on a ProMaster City wagon?

Yes — rear and side windows can be wrapped with perforated window film that allows visibility from inside while displaying graphics from outside. We use 50/50 dot pattern film as standard. It's a popular option for service businesses that want full coverage graphics without sacrificing rear visibility.
