After the Wrap — Care, Warranty, and Reorders

Every Ram ProMaster City wrap we install comes with a 2-year workmanship warranty, covering issues such as lifting, bubbling, edge peeling, and installation defects. This is distinct from the manufacturer's vinyl warranty, where Avery Dennison and 3M offer their own film performance warranties, typically ranging from 5–7 years for outdoor use on your ProMaster City.

To maximize the lifespan of your Ram ProMaster City wrap, always hand wash with mild soap and water. Avoid automated brush car washes as they can scratch the vinyl and lift edges. When using pressure washers, keep them below 1,200 PSI and at least 12 inches away from panel edges. Promptly address bird droppings and tree sap, as these acidic compounds can etch into the vinyl if left untreated.

When you expand your fleet with additional Ram ProMaster City vans in the future, your branding files are securely stored with us. We never discard fleet artwork. This means reorders for your new ProMaster City vans can go into production much faster, as the initial design approval is already complete.
