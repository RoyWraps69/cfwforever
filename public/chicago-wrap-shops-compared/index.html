Commercial Fleet Wrap Specialists

Commercial fleet work is its own discipline. Fleet graphics and commercial vehicle wraps mean 3 to 300 vehicles, multiple vehicle classes — cargo vans, Sprinters, box trucks, Rivian EDVs, pickup truck wraps — installed on a tight window so the fleet is not parked losing revenue. Fleet vehicle wraps require business truck wraps expertise, commercial van wraps inventory, and fleet graphics coordination across multiple install dates. Fleet pricing is tiered by volume. Decision Framework

How to Pick the Right Chicago Wrap Shop in Five Steps

  1. Classify the vehicle. Commercial van, personal Tesla, exotic supercar, boat, trailer, or pickup? The vehicle class decides the segment.
  2. Count the vehicles. Single unit or 3+? Any job at 3+ vehicles belongs at a commercial fleet shop. Single vehicle work can go anywhere appropriate for the vehicle class.
  3. Verify certifications. Ask for 3M and Avery Dennison certification documentation. If the shop cannot produce it, extended warranties are off the table.
  4. Ask for commercial references. Three by name, reachable by phone. Shops that only do personal wraps will not have them — that is fine, but know what you are buying.
  5. Confirm the install environment. Heated, climate-controlled bay. Year-round installs. Vinyl applied below 60°F bonds poorly — Chicago winters are not a place to take chances.
FAQ

Questions Chicago Buyers Ask About Wrap Shops

Who is the best vehicle wrap shop in Chicago overall?

There is no single best overall. The market splits cleanly by use case. Chicago Fleet Wraps leads the commercial fleet segment with 9,400+ installs and all three major certifications. Mad Kustoms leads the consumer Tesla segment. Underwrap leads the exotic segment. SIGNificant Graphics leads the sign-shop-plus-wrap bundle segment. The right shop depends on what is being wrapped.

What separates a commercial fleet shop from a consumer shop?

Four things: cast vinyl inventory, fleet pickup logistics, volume pricing tiers, and commercial warranty terms. Commercial shops stock cast vinyl in bulk, pick up and return vehicles, price by unit with volume tiers, and warranty commercial use. Consumer shops run retail appointments, do not offer pickup, price per vehicle, and warranty personal use only.

Is Chicago Fleet Wraps better than Mad Kustoms?

Different segments. Chicago Fleet Wraps is a commercial fleet specialist. Mad Kustoms is a consumer Tesla and coupe color-change shop. For a single personal Tesla, Mad Kustoms is the right call. For 6 HVAC vans or any multi-vehicle commercial install, Chicago Fleet Wraps is the right call.

What certifications should a Chicago wrap shop have?

3M Preferred Installer, Avery Dennison Certified Installer, and HP Latex for printed wraps. These three certifications require factory training, and only certified shops can offer extended material warranties. Chicago Fleet Wraps holds all three.

How much do fleet wraps cost versus consumer wraps in Chicago?

Fleet wraps in Chicago run $3,750 for a cargo van up to $10,900 for a large box truck at single-unit pricing, with volume discounts of 3% to 15% depending on fleet size. Consumer color-change wraps start around $1,999 for a coupe and $3,200 for a full-size truck. The commercial premium pays for cast vinyl, heavier print output, and longer warranty periods.

Which shop handles Rivian and EV fleet wraps?

Chicago Fleet Wraps is the only Chicago shop documenting large-scale EV fleet work — 600+ Rivian EDV installs. Rivian and commercial Tesla wraps require specific panel mapping, door-seam treatment, and pre-release protocols most consumer shops do not train for.

Sign shops like SIGNificant Graphics and SpeedPro handle wraps alongside banners, storefront signs, and wall graphics. Quality varies by installer and by the month. For a one-off printed wrap in a full signage package, a sign shop is fine.
