Navigating Illinois Window Tinting Laws

Adhering to Illinois state regulations for window tinting is paramount, and Chicago Fleet Wraps ensures full compliance with every installation. For sedans, the front side windows and windshield are subject to specific Visible Light Transmittance (VLT) percentages, while rear side and back windows have more flexibility. We provide expert guidance to help you choose legal and effective tint levels.

Our team is well-versed in the nuances of Illinois tint laws, including requirements for commercial vehicles and SUVs, which often have different allowances. We use state-of-the-art VLT meters to verify compliance, giving you peace of mind that your tinted windows will pass inspection anywhere from Evanston to Orland Park, avoiding potential fines and legal issues.

Maintenance and Longevity of Your Window Tint

Maintaining your newly tinted windows is straightforward, ensuring their longevity and pristine appearance. We recommend waiting at least 3-5 days before rolling down windows to allow the adhesive to fully cure. For cleaning, simply use a soft cloth and a non-ammonia-based cleaner; harsh chemicals can damage the film and void warranties.

With proper care, our high-quality window tints are designed to last for many years, resisting bubbling, peeling, and fading. Chicago Fleet Wraps stands behind its workmanship with a comprehensive 2-year warranty, providing an extra layer of assurance for your investment. This commitment to quality ensures your tint looks great for the long haul, even with Chicago's unpredictable weather.

Pricing for Professional Window Tinting in Chicagoland

The cost of professional window tinting varies based on several factors, including the type of vehicle, the number of windows, and the specific film technology chosen (e.g., carbon vs. ceramic). For a standard sedan, pricing typically ranges from $250 to $550 for a complete tint. SUVs and trucks may range from $350 to $700 due to larger glass areas.

Fleet tinting projects are quoted individually, with potential volume discounts for multiple vehicles. Premium ceramic films, offering superior heat rejection and UV protection, are usually at the higher end of the spectrum. We provide transparent, no-obligation quotes after assessing your specific needs, ensuring you understand all costs involved before any work begins at our Chicago facility.
