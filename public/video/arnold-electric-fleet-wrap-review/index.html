Investment in Marketing: Understanding Wrap Costs

When considering a fleet wrap, businesses like Arnold Electric often inquire about the investment. While costs vary based on vehicle type, wrap coverage (full vs. partial), and design complexity, a full wrap for a standard van or truck typically ranges from $3,000 to $5,000. This includes premium materials like 3M IJ180-CV3 or Avery Dennison MPI 1105 and professional installation.

Partial wraps, which cover less surface area but still offer significant impact, can range from $1,800 to $3,000. These figures represent a highly cost-effective marketing solution when compared to the continuous expense of traditional advertising. The upfront investment yields years of mobile advertising, as Arnold Electric has experienced, turning their vehicles into productive assets rather than just transportation.
