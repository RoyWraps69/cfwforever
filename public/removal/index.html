Our Removal Process

  1. Vehicle inspection — assess wrap age, vinyl type, and paint condition
  2. Controlled heating — infrared heat softens adhesive for clean release
  3. Slow peeling — vinyl removed at optimal angle to prevent paint pull
  4. Adhesive removal — citrus-based cleaners dissolve residue without damaging clear coat
  5. Surface inspection — verify paint condition and prep for re-wrap if applicable
  6. Delivery — vehicle returned clean and wrap-free

When to Remove a Wrap

Common reasons businesses request wrap removal in Chicago:

  • Rebranding — company name, logo, or colors have changed
  • Lease return — vehicle must be returned in original condition
  • Design update — refreshing fleet graphics with a new look
  • Vehicle sale — removing graphics before selling or trading in
  • Wrap age — vinyl is past its rated life and beginning to show wear
  • Damage repair — body work requires wrap removal before paint repair

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does wrap removal cost?

$500–$1,500 for cars, $800–$3,500 for box trucks. Pricing depends on vehicle size, vinyl age, and type. Adhesive cleanup included.

Will removal damage my paint?

Not when done properly. We use controlled heat and slow peeling. Cast vinyl (Avery, 3M) removes cleanly. Calendered vinyl and wraps over 7 years old may require extra care.

How long does removal take?

1–2 days for a car or van. 2–3 days for a box truck. Same-day available for partial graphics and decals.

Can you remove and re-wrap in one visit?

Yes. Removal + re-wrap is one of our most common services. We remove, prep, and install the new wrap in a single visit.

Do you remove wraps from leased vehicles?

Yes. Lease-return wrap removal is a common service. We ensure the vehicle is returned in original condition.

What about old, sun-baked wraps?

Wraps past 7+ years may be more difficult to remove but we handle them regularly. Older vinyl becomes brittle and requires slower, more controlled removal. Price may be higher for aged wraps.

Do you offer fleet wrap removal?

Yes. Fleet removal with volume pricing and free pickup. Common for rebranding, lease fleet returns, or seasonal design changes.
