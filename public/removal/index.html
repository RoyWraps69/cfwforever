Our Removal Process

  1. Vehicle inspection — assess wrap age, vinyl type, and paint condition
  2. Controlled heating — infrared heat softens adhesive for clean release
  3. Slow peeling — vinyl removed at optimal angle to prevent paint pull
  4. Adhesive removal — citrus-based cleaners dissolve residue without damaging clear coat

  6. Delivery — vehicle returned clean and wrap-free

The Wrap Removal Process: What to Expect

Our professional wrap removal process begins with a thorough inspection of your vehicle and the existing wrap. This allows our team to assess the vinyl type, its condition, and the underlying paint. We then strategically apply controlled heat to soften the vinyl and adhesive, making it pliable and easier to remove in large sections, minimizing residue.

Following the primary removal of the vinyl, our technicians meticulously clean the vehicle's surface to eliminate any remaining adhesive residue. We use specialized, paint-safe cleaning agents that effectively dissolve lingering stickiness without damaging the clear coat. The result is a pristine surface, ready for its next application or simply to shine on its own.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does vehicle wrap removal typically take at Chicago Fleet Wraps?

The duration varies based on vehicle size and wrap condition. A standard car might take 4-8 hours, while a large commercial truck or Sprinter van could require 1-2 full days for complete, meticulous removal and adhesive cleanup.

Will removing my vehicle wrap damage the original paint underneath?

No, when performed professionally by Chicago Fleet Wraps, wrap removal will not damage your vehicle's original paint. We use specialized techniques and tools to ensure a damage-free process, preserving the factory finish.

What if there's a lot of adhesive residue left after the wrap is removed?

Our process includes thorough adhesive removal. After the vinyl is peeled, our technicians meticulously clean the surface with professional-grade, paint-safe solvents to eliminate all residue, leaving the paint smooth and clean.

Can Chicago Fleet Wraps remove old or faded wraps that have been on for years?

Yes, we specialize in removing wraps of all ages and conditions, including those that are old, faded, or cracked due to prolonged exposure to Chicago's weather. Our expertise ensures even challenging removals are handled effectively.

Do I need to wash my vehicle before bringing it in for wrap removal?

While not strictly required, a relatively clean vehicle helps. Excessive dirt can hinder the initial assessment and make the removal process slightly longer. A basic wash is appreciated but not essential for our service.

What is the typical cost range for vehicle wrap removal in Chicago?

The cost for wrap removal in Chicago generally ranges from $400 for smaller vehicles to $2,500+ for larger commercial trucks or vans. Factors like vehicle size, wrap age, and vinyl quality influence the final price.

Can Chicago Fleet Wraps remove wraps from specialty vehicles like food trucks or EVs?

Absolutely. Our experienced team is equipped to handle wrap removal from a wide array of specialty vehicles, including food trucks, electric vehicles (EVs), box trucks, and Sprinter vans, ensuring the same quality and care.
