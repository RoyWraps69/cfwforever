Beyond Aesthetics: The Durability of Premium Delivery Vehicle Wraps

For delivery vehicles that endure constant exposure to Chicago's diverse urban and suburban environments, durability is not just a feature, it's a necessity. Our exclusive use of Avery Dennison MPI 1105 and 3M IJ180-CV3 cast vinyl ensures your delivery fleet graphics withstand daily wear and tear, UV exposure, and frequent washing. Protecting Your Investment: Maintenance & Longevity of Delivery Wraps

Maintaining the pristine appearance and extending the lifespan of your delivery fleet wraps is straightforward. We recommend regular hand washing with mild, pH-neutral detergents, avoiding abrasive brushes or high-pressure washers, which can compromise the wrap's integrity. Promptly removing bird droppings, tree sap, and other contaminants is crucial, especially for vehicles frequently parked or idling near the Chicago River or in tree-lined suburban streets.

With proper maintenance, your CFW delivery fleet graphics, backed by our 2-year workmanship warranty, will continue to perform optimally for 5-7 years, protecting your vehicle's original paint and providing consistent brand exposure. This longevity ensures your marketing investment continues to pay dividends, establishing your presence and credibility throughout the vast Chicagoland area, from urban cores to far-flung distribution centers.
