Why Delivery Companies Wrap Their Fleets

Delivery vehicles spend more time on the road than almost any other commercial vehicle type. A single wrapped delivery van running daily routes through Chicago neighborhoods, suburbs, and expressways generates more advertising impressions than a prime billboard on the Kennedy — at a fraction of the cost. Your drivers are already covering the miles. Wrapping your fleet turns every delivery into a branding event.

Major delivery and logistics companies invest in fleet wraps because the math is undeniable: at $0.04–$0.07 per thousand impressions, fleet wraps deliver the lowest cost-per-impression of any advertising medium — 100x cheaper than Google Ads, 50x cheaper than radio, and 10x cheaper than billboards.
