What Affects Cargo Van Wrap Cost in Chicago

Four variables drive price: coverage percentage (full vs partial), van size (compact vs full-size vs high-roof), design complexity (cut vinyl vs full-color print), and fleet volume. Every quote from Chicago Fleet Wraps is itemized — you see exactly what you're paying for.

Full Wrap vs Partial Wrap — Which Is Worth It?

A full wrap covers 100% of the van's painted surface. A partial wrap covers 40–60%, usually the sides and rear doors. For brand visibility on the road, full wraps generate 30,000+ daily impressions per vehicle. For tight budgets, a high-quality partial wrap with your logo, phone number, and color scheme still outperforms any other form of local advertising at this price point.

How Long Does a Cargo Van Wrap Last?

Cast vinyl wraps last 5–7 years with proper care. Hand wash only — no automated brush car washes. No high-pressure washing on seams and edges. Chicago Fleet Wraps uses cast vinyl exclusively — never calendered vinyl, which shrinks and cracks within 2–3 years.
