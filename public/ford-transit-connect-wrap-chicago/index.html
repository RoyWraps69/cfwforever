Neither of these is a cheap material. After the Wrap — Care, Warranty, and Reorders

Every Ford Transit Connect wrap we install comes with a 2-year workmanship warranty, covering issues such as lifting, bubbling, edge peeling, and any installation defects. This warranty is distinct from the manufacturer's vinyl warranty, with both Avery Dennison and 3M providing their own film performance warranties of 5–7 years for outdoor use on your Transit Connect.

To maximize the lifespan of your Ford Transit Connect wrap, hand wash it using mild soap and water. Avoid automated brush car washes, as they can scratch and lift edges. When using pressure washers, keep them below 1,200 PSI and at least 12 inches away from panel edges. Promptly address bird droppings and tree sap, as these acidic compounds can etch into the vinyl if left on your Transit Connect.

When you expand your fleet by adding more Ford Transit Connect vans in the future, your brand files are permanently stored with us. We never delete fleet artwork. This means reorders for additional Transit Connect wraps go into production much faster, as the design approval process is already complete. We simply adapt your existing brand standards to the new Ford Transit Connect vehicle template and submit it for a quick review.
