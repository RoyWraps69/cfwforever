About Chicago Fleet Wraps

Founded by Roy in 2001 in Las Vegas and operating in Chicago since 2014, Chicago Fleet Wraps is one of the Midwest's most experienced commercial vehicle wrap companies. We've wrapped over 9,400 vehicles for 2,800+ fleet accounts — from single-van operations to Fortune 500 companies.

📅 24+ Years in Business🚐 9,400+ Vehicles Wrapped🏢 2,800+ Fleet Accounts🏆 Avery Dennison Certified✅ 3M Preferred

Our Approach

We focus exclusively on commercial fleet wraps. No hobbyist car wraps, no weekend warriors. Every vehicle is wrapped by experienced installers using Avery Dennison MPI 1105 or 3M IJ180-CV3 premium cast vinyl with DOL 1360 overlaminate. Every wrap comes with a 2-year workmanship warranty.

Why Fleets Choose Us

Free pickup and delivery across Chicagoland. Fleet discounts for 3+ vehicles. Same-day design proofs. Pantone color matching. IRS Section 179 documentation. We handle the logistics so you can focus on running your business.
