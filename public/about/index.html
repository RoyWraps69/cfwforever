The Origin Story — From Las Vegas Exotics to Chicago Fleets

In 2001, Roy launched Las Vegas Car Wraps in the heart of the Strip's high-roller culture. Las Vegas was ground zero for exotic cars, and Roy quickly built a reputation as the go-to wrapper for the city's most demanding clientele. Ferraris, Lamborghinis, McLarens, Bentleys, Rolls-Royces — if it had a six-figure price tag and an owner who expected perfection, it ended up in Roy's shop.

Over 13 years in Vegas, Roy and his team wrapped thousands of exotic and luxury vehicles, mastering the precision techniques required for complex curves, carbon fiber panels, and multi-piece body kits. That exacting standard — where a single bubble or misaligned seam is unacceptable — became the DNA of the business.

In 2014, Roy sold Las Vegas Car Wraps and moved his family home to Chicago. But he didn't retire. He saw an underserved market: commercial fleet wraps. Chicago's contractor, HVAC, plumbing, electrical, delivery, and food service industries were running thousands of plain white vans and box trucks — rolling billboards going completely to waste.

Roy pivoted. He applied the same obsessive quality standards from wrapping half-million-dollar supercars to wrapping cargo vans, Sprinters, box trucks, and pickup trucks. The difference was immediate. Fleet owners who had been burned by cheap wrap shops — peeling vinyl, color mismatches, two-month turnaround times — found a partner who treated a $3,150 cargo van wrap with the same care as a $15,000 Lamborghini wrap.

"The techniques don't change between a Lamborghini and a Sprinter van. The curves are different, but the standard is exactly the same — zero bubbles, perfect edges, seams you can't find with your fingernail."

— Roy, Founder

Our Facility — 4711 N Lamon Ave, Chicago

Every wrap is installed at our dedicated facility in Chicago's Old Irving Park / Jefferson Park corridor. We do not subcontract installations, and we do not install wraps in parking lots or at client sites. All work is performed in a controlled environment designed specifically for vehicle wraps.

Climate-Controlled Bay

Maintained at 70°F year-round. Proper temperature is critical — cold causes vinyl adhesion failure, heat causes overstretching. Consistent temperature ensures maximum bond strength.

LED Overhead Lighting

Full-spectrum LED shop lights eliminate shadows and reveal imperfections invisible under fluorescent or natural light. Every seam, edge, and panel is inspected under controlled lighting.

Dust-Free Environment

Sealed bay with filtered air circulation. Dust and debris trapped under vinyl create permanent bumps — our clean environment prevents contamination before it happens.

HP Latex Printing

In-house HP Latex wide-format printers produce vibrant, eco-solvent graphics with zero odor. No outsourced printing — design to installation happens under one roof.

Vehicle Staging Area

On-site staging area allows us to hold multiple fleet vehicles simultaneously. We can process 3–4 vehicles per week without scheduling conflicts.

Expressway Access

2 minutes from the Kennedy Expressway (I-90/94). Direct access to O'Hare, downtown, and every Chicagoland suburb for fast pickup and delivery runs.
