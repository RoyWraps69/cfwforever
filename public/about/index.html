About Chicago Fleet Wraps

Chicago Fleet Wraps is a family-owned commercial vehicle wrap company founded by Roy in 2001. What started in a Las Vegas garage wrapping Ferraris, Lamborghinis, and exotic supercars has evolved into one of the Midwest's most experienced fleet wrap operations — with 9,400+ vehicles wrapped, 2,800+ fleet accounts, and a reputation built on craftsmanship, honesty, and results that drive real business.

Our Story

Roy, our founder, began his career in vehicle wraps in 2001 in Las Vegas, Nevada. What started as a small operation focused on high-end exotic cars quickly grew into a reputable business known for its quality and attention to detail. In 2014, Roy relocated to Chicago, bringing his expertise and passion for vehicle wraps to the Midwest.

Since then, Chicago Fleet Wraps has become a leading provider of commercial vehicle wraps in the region. We've had the privilege of working with a diverse range of clients, from small businesses to large corporations, helping them enhance their brand visibility and make a lasting impression on the road.

Our Mission

At Chicago Fleet Wraps, our mission is simple: to provide our clients with top-quality vehicle wraps that exceed their expectations. We are committed to using the best materials, employing skilled technicians, and delivering exceptional customer service. We believe that a well-designed and professionally installed vehicle wrap can be a powerful marketing tool, and we're dedicated to helping our clients achieve their business goals through effective visual communication.
