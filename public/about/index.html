About Chicago Fleet Wraps

24 Years. 9,400+ Vehicles. One Shop on Lamon Ave.

Chicago Fleet Wraps started in Las Vegas in 2001 and moved to Chicago in 2014. How We Operate

Cast Vinyl Only. No Exceptions.

We use Avery Dennison MPI 1105 and 3M IJ180-CV3. Both are cast vinyl — the commercial grade that conforms to vehicle contours, holds color under UV, and removes cleanly. We have never used calendered vinyl on a commercial install and never will.

Climate-Controlled Year Round.

Vinyl applied below 60°F bonds poorly. Chicago winters run from November through March. Every install at our shop happens in a heated, temperature-controlled bay regardless of what's happening outside. That's not a selling point — it's a basic requirement we've always met.

Free Fleet Pickup.

Fleet accounts with 3 or more vehicles get free pickup and delivery throughout the six-county Chicagoland area. Your drivers don't lose work time getting vehicles to us. We coordinate the pickup schedule around your operation.

2-Year Workmanship Warranty.

Every install comes with a 2-year workmanship warranty covering installation defects, plus the Avery and 3M manufacturer material warranties on top of that. If it fails because of how it was installed, we fix it. Simple as that.
