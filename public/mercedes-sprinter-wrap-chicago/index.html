After the Wrap — Care, Warranty, and Reorders

Every Mercedes-Benz Sprinter wrap we install includes a 2-year workmanship warranty, covering issues such as lifting, bubbling, edge peeling, and any installation defects. This is distinct from the vinyl manufacturer's warranty; both Avery Dennison and 3M provide their own film performance warranties, typically ranging from 5–7 years for outdoor usage.

To maximize the longevity of your Mercedes-Benz Sprinter wrap: always hand wash with mild soap and water, avoid automated brush car washes which can scratch and lift edges, keep pressure washer nozzles at least 12 inches from panel edges and below 1,200 PSI, and promptly address bird droppings and tree sap as their acidic compounds can etch into the vinyl if left untreated for extended periods.

Should you expand your fleet with additional Mercedes-Benz Sprinter vans months or even years down the line, your specific brand files are permanently archived with us. We never delete fleet artwork. This means reorders for new Sprinter wraps enter production much faster, as the initial design approval is already complete. We simply adapt your existing brand standard to the new vehicle template and then submit it for final production.
