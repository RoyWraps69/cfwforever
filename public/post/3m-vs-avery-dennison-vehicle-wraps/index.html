3M vs Avery Dennison Vehicle Wraps

As a shop that installs both Avery Dennison and 3M films daily, here's our honest comparison after 9,400+ installations:

Avery Dennison MPI 1105

  • Our preferred film for commercial fleet wraps
  • Exceptional conformability around rivets and curves
  • Easy Dot air-release adhesive for bubble-free installs
  • 5–7 year outdoor durability
  • Best color consistency across large fleet runs

3M IJ180-CV3

  • Industry-standard cast vinyl
  • Comply v3 adhesive with micro air channels
  • Excellent for color change wraps with wider color selection
  • 5–7 year outdoor durability
  • Slightly wider range of specialty finishes

Our Recommendation

For commercial fleet wraps, we lean Avery Dennison for consistency and conformability. For color change wraps, 3M often has more specialty finishes available. Both are premium cast vinyl with identical lifespan ratings. You can't go wrong with either.
