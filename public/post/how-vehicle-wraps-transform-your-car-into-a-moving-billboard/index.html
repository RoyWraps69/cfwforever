How Vehicle Wraps Transform Your Car Into a Moving Billboard

A single wrapped vehicle generates between 30,000 and 70,000 daily impressions according to the Outdoor Advertising Association of America. That makes vehicle wraps one of the most cost-effective advertising channels available to small and mid-size businesses.

Impressions That Travel With You

Unlike a static billboard locked to one location, a wrapped vehicle moves through neighborhoods, highways, and parking lots — reaching new audiences every day. For Chicago businesses, that means exposure across the entire metro area without recurring media costs.

Cost Per Impression

Vehicle wraps deliver a cost-per-thousand impressions (CPM) of just $0.04 to $0.15, compared to $3–$8 for radio and $10–$20 for print. A one-time investment in a quality fleet wrap pays dividends for 5–7 years.

24/7 Advertising

Your wrapped vehicle advertises even when parked. A branded van in a residential driveway or commercial parking lot generates impressions around the clock — no ad spend required.

Professional Brand Image

A professionally wrapped vehicle signals credibility and permanence. Customers trust businesses that invest in their brand presentation, and a clean, cohesive fleet wrap reinforces that trust at every touchpoint.
