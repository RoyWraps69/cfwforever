Frequently Asked Questions

How long do vehicle wraps last?

Avery Dennison MPI 1105 and 3M IJ180-CV3 are rated 5–7 years outdoor. With proper care, Chicago fleet wraps regularly hit 6–7 years even with road salt and winter exposure.

Will a wrap damage my paint?

No. Zero verified paint damage claims in 10+ years. Wraps protect factory paint from UV and minor abrasion.

Are wraps tax deductible?

Yes — 100% deductible under IRS Section 179 as a business advertising expense.
