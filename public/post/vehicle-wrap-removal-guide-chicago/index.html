Frequently Asked Questions

How much does vehicle wrap removal cost in Chicago?

Professional wrap removal at CFW costs $400–$500 for a cargo van and $600–$800 for a box truck. Vinyl removed within its rated lifespan removes at the lower end of the price range. Aged vinyl beyond 8 years requires more labor.

How do you remove a vehicle wrap without damaging the paint?

Use a heat gun at 140–180°F at 4–6 inch distance to soften the adhesive, peel at 15–20 degrees from the panel surface, then clean residue with 70% isopropyl alcohol on a lint-free cloth. CFW has zero verified paint damage claims from professional removal.

Can I remove a vehicle wrap myself?

Yes, but heat is non-negotiable. Use a heat gun at 140–180°F and peel at a low angle. Cold removal — pulling without heat — is the primary cause of paint damage in DIY removals.

How long does wrap removal take?

Cargo van removal: 4–6 hours. Box truck: 8–12 hours. Vinyl age is the primary variable: fresh cast vinyl may remove in 3–4 hours; vinyl beyond 8 years requires chemical softener and extended time.

Will the paint be damaged when my wrap is removed?

Not with professional removal. Cast vinyl removed within its rated lifespan using correct heat and peel technique does not damage factory paint. Factory paint under a properly installed and removed wrap is typically in better condition than exposed areas on the same vehicle.
