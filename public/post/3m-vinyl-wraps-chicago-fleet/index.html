Comparing 3M IJ180-CV3 with Avery Dennison MPI 1105: An Expert Perspective

While both 3M IJ180-CV3 and Avery Dennison MPI 1105 are top-tier cast vinyl films, each has unique characteristics that make them ideal for specific applications. 3M IJ180-CV3 is often lauded for its robust durability and exceptional conformability, making it a go-to for complex vehicle contours and long-term fleet applications, especially those facing harsh Chicago weather conditions. Its Comply™ adhesive is highly forgiving during installation, a benefit for intricate designs.

Avery Dennison MPI 1105, on the other hand, is celebrated for its vivid print quality and ease of repositionability during application, which can be advantageous for highly detailed graphics. Both materials offer outstanding longevity and paint protection. At Chicago Fleet Wraps, we expertly guide you through the selection process, ensuring the chosen premium film, whether 3M or Avery, perfectly aligns with your fleet's specific needs, branding goals, and budget, delivering a superior result for your vehicles operating from Wicker Park to Joliet.
