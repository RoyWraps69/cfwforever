3M Vinyl Wraps: Best Choice for Your Chicago Fleet

Chicago Fleet Wraps offers top 3M vinyl wraps in Chicago, IL. Here's why 3M remains a go-to material for commercial fleets:

3M IJ180-CV3 — The Fleet Standard

3M's IJ180-CV3 is a cast vinyl with Comply v3 adhesive featuring micro air channels for bubble-free application. It's rated for 5–7 years outdoor and handles Chicago's extreme temperature swings without shrinking or cracking.

Durability in Chicago Weather

From -20°F winters to 95°F summers, 3M cast vinyl maintains adhesion and color integrity. Combined with DOL 1360 overlaminate, it resists UV fading, road salt, and chemical exposure.

Fleet Protection

Beyond branding, 3M wraps add a protective layer against rock chips, minor scratches, and UV damage to your factory paint. When it's time to sell or re-brand, the wrap removes cleanly, preserving resale value.
