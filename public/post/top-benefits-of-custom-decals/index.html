Top Benefits of Custom Decals for Your Fleet Vehicles

Custom fleet decals are specially designed graphics that can be customized with your company's logo, branding, and contact information. Here's why they're worth the investment:

1. Cost-Effective Branding

Decals and partial wraps start at a fraction of a full wrap's cost while still delivering thousands of daily impressions. A logo, phone number, and website on both sides of a van can generate calls from day one.

2. Professional Image

Branded vehicles signal legitimacy. Homeowners are far more likely to open their door to a service tech arriving in a professionally branded vehicle than an unmarked one.

3. 24/7 Advertising

Your fleet works for you even when parked. A branded van parked at a job site, in a driveway, or in a parking lot is a stationary billboard seen by every passerby.

4. Easy to Update

Changed your phone number? Added a new service? Decals can be removed and replaced without affecting the vehicle's paint. Full wraps require more effort to update, but individual decal panels are quick and affordable to swap.
