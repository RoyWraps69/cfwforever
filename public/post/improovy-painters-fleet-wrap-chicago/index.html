The Project

Improovy Painters operates a growing residential and commercial painting fleet across Chicago and the suburbs. They needed consistent branding across 5 cargo vans — vehicles driving through every neighborhood on the North and Northwest Sides every day, but looking like unmarked work trucks.

We wrapped all 5 in the same production window using Avery Dennison MPI 1105 — the fleet standard for multi-vehicle programs where consistency across production runs matters. Every van was prepped identically: surface cleaned with isopropyl, inspected for paint defects, wrapped in our climate-controlled Portage Park facility.

The result: 5 vans that look like 1 fleet — uniform branding, uniform finish, uniform lifespan.
