How Much Does a Car Wrap Cost in Chicago?

Vehicle wrap pricing depends on vehicle size, coverage level, and material quality. Here's what to expect in the Chicago market with premium cast vinyl:

Vehicle TypeFull WrapPartial Wrap
Sedan / Coupe$2,800 – $3,500$1,400 – $2,200
SUV / Crossover$3,200 – $4,200$1,600 – $2,800
Cargo Van$2,800 – $4,500$1,500 – $2,800
Sprinter Van$3,500 – $5,200$2,200 – $3,500
Box Truck 16–22 ft$4,500 – $7,500$2,800 – $4,500

These prices include design, print, premium cast vinyl (Avery Dennison MPI 1105 or 3M IJ180-CV3), UV overlaminate, and certified installation. Fleet discounts: 5% off 3–4 vehicles, 10% off 5–9, 15% off 10+.

Beware of shops quoting under $2,000 for a full car wrap — they're almost certainly using calendered vinyl that will fail within 1–2 years.
