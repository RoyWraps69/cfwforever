The Challenge: HVAC Fleet Rebrand

Bratcher Heating and Air had been running die-cut vinyl lettering on their HVAC service vans. After years of Chicago winters and the thermal cycling HVAC vans go through — hot engine, cold overnight temps, repeat — the decals were lifting and yellowing. For an HVAC contractor asking clients to trust them with their mechanical systems, a truck that looks unmaintained sends exactly the wrong signal.

The choice to go gloss was deliberate. Gloss reads as clean in residential driveways even with light road film. HVAC vans sit in front of homes while technicians work inside — every neighbor watching is a potential customer. A high-gloss wrap in brand colors makes the right impression at point-of-need.

Avery MPI 1105 gloss delivers a clear, deep finish that holds without yellowing. Same base film as the matte — same conformability, same edge performance — different overlaminate. The result: a fleet that looks like a modern, professional HVAC company, not a company still running decals from 2015.
