What Affects Vehicle Wrap Cost?

  • Vehicle Size — Larger vehicles require more material and labor. A 26-ft box truck uses 5x the vinyl of a sedan.
  • Coverage Level — Full wraps cost more than partial wraps, which cost more than lettering. Partial wrap details →
  • Design Complexity — Simple logo placement vs. full custom illustration affects design time.
  • Material Choice — Premium cast vinyl (what we use) costs more than calendered vinyl but lasts 3x longer.
  • Fleet Size — Volume discounts reduce per-vehicle costs significantly.
  • Special Finishes — Chrome, color-shift, and metallic films carry a premium over standard gloss/matte.

Full Wrap vs. Partial Wrap Pricing

Full wraps cover every visible panel for maximum brand impact. They generate the most impressions and deliver the best long-term ROI. Full wraps are the standard for fleet branding programs.

Partial wraps cover strategic panels — typically sides, rear, and sometimes hood — at 40–60% of full wrap cost. They're ideal for businesses starting their fleet branding or working with tighter budgets. Most partial wraps deliver 60–80% of the visual impact of a full wrap.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a vehicle wrap cost in Chicago?

Vehicle wrap costs range from $1,200 for partial wraps to $10,000+ for large box trucks. Sedans: $2,500–$4,000. Cargo vans: $2,800–$4,500. Sprinter vans: $3,500–$5,200. Box trucks: $4,200–$10,000.

What factors determine the price of a vehicle wrap?

Vehicle size, coverage level (full vs partial), design complexity, material choice, and fleet quantity all affect pricing. We use only premium cast vinyl — no cheap calendered material.

Is a vehicle wrap cheaper than paint?

Yes. A professional paint job costs $5,000–$15,000+ and is permanent. A premium vinyl wrap costs $2,500–$5,200, lasts 5–7 years, protects the paint, and can be removed without damage.

Are vehicle wraps tax deductible?

Yes — vehicle wraps used for business advertising are 100% tax deductible under IRS Section 179. The full cost can be deducted in the year of installation.
