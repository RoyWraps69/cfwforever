Schaumburg Fleet Wrap FAQ

Do you serve businesses in the Woodfield area?

Yes — we wrap fleets for companies throughout Schaumburg, including the Woodfield Mall district, Golf Road corridor, Meacham Road business parks, and all surrounding areas. Free pickup and delivery included.

How quickly can you wrap our Schaumburg fleet?

We phase fleet orders to minimize vehicle downtime. A single cargo van wrap takes 1–2 days. For multi-vehicle fleets, we typically process 2–3 vehicles per week, returning each one as soon as it's complete so your Schaumburg operations aren't disrupted.

What's the ROI on fleet wraps in the Schaumburg area?

A wrapped vehicle in Schaumburg generates an estimated 30,000–70,000 impressions per day at a cost-per-impression under $0.04. Compare that to $15–$25 CPM for digital ads. Over a 5-year wrap lifespan, the cost works out to roughly $3–$6 per day — less than a coffee.

Do you handle large corporate fleet wrap programs for Schaumburg companies?

Yes — we regularly handle fleet programs of 20–200+ vehicles for Schaumburg-based corporations. Large fleet programs include dedicated project management, phased installation scheduling to minimize operational downtime, and volume discounts of up to 15% for fleets of 10+ vehicles.

What's the difference between a partial wrap and a full wrap for Schaumburg fleets?

A full wrap covers the entire vehicle surface and delivers maximum brand impact and paint protection. A partial wrap covers the rear and sides (typically 50–70% of the vehicle) at a lower cost. For Schaumburg corporate fleets, we often recommend full wraps for customer-facing vehicles and partial wraps for internal fleet vehicles.
