BACKGROUND & EXPERTISE

Roy has spent his entire professional career in commercial vehicle graphics. He started wrapping vehicles in Las Vegas in 2001, building Chicago Fleet Wraps from a single-van operation into a multi-crew shop handling complex fleet programs. In 2014, he relocated to Chicago — a city with a commercial vehicle density that rewards high-quality wrap operations.

His work spans every category of commercial vehicle: standard cargo vans, extended and high-roof Sprinters, 16- to 26-foot box trucks, semi trailers, electric vehicles across every major platform, boats, and specialty equipment. He works hands-on across installation, design review, print quality control, and client consultation — not a shop owner who left the bay years ago.

Roy's technical expertise covers the full workflow: surface preparation, IPA wipe protocol, cast vinyl selection, HP Latex print calibration, ICC profile creation for Avery MPI 1105 and 3M IJ180-CV3, SAi Flexi RIP operation, and Epson SureColor resin ink workflows. He knows the difference between a wrap that holds for 7 years and one that lifts at 8 months — and has built every CFW installation protocol around that knowledge.

Zero verified paint damage claims in 10+ years of operation. That is the standard Roy holds every install to.

CAREER TIMELINE
2001

Founded Chicago Fleet Wraps — Las Vegas, NV

Started wrapping commercial vehicles in Las Vegas with a focus on fleet work. Built core installation protocols that remain the operational standard today: surface prep, temperature verification, cast-vinyl-only policy, post-heat adhesive activation.

2006

HP Latex Certification

Completed HP Latex certification as HP transitioned the wide-format printing industry to water-based latex ink chemistry. CFW adopted HP Latex early — the print quality and color accuracy advantages for fleet work were immediate and significant.

2010

Avery Dennison & 3M Authorizations

Earned Avery Dennison authorized installer status and 3M authorized installer status. These certifications required demonstrated proficiency on both manufacturers' premium cast vinyl materials and formalized CFW's position as a quality-tier installer.

2014

Relocated to Chicago

Moved Chicago Fleet Wraps to Chicago, IL — 4711 N Lamon Ave #7, Chicago 60630. The Chicago commercial vehicle market — density of service fleets, expressway traffic volume, Fortune 500 regional headquarters — justified the move. Fleet account growth accelerated significantly in year one.

EV Wrap Specialization — Rivian Fleet Program

Developed specialized installation protocols for electric vehicles — particularly Rivian R1T, R1S, and EDV commercial vans. EV-specific challenges: non-standard body contours, flush door handles, exposed charging port locations, and heat-sensitive underbody panels. 600+ Rivians wrapped to date, making CFW Illinois' largest EV wrap installer.
