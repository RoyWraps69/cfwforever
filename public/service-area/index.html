Service Area — Common Questions

Do you pick up fleet vehicles for wrap installation?

Yes — free pickup and delivery for fleet accounts of 3 or more vehicles, anywhere in Chicagoland. We coordinate around your dispatch hours so the trucks come to us when you don't need them on the road.

How far from your shop will you travel?

Our shop is at 4711 N Lamon Ave on Chicago's Northwest Side. Free pickup covers the entire metro: Naperville and Aurora to the west, Joliet and Tinley Park to the south, Evanston and the North Shore to the north, Schaumburg and the northwest suburbs. Inside the I-294 / I-355 corridor, we come to you.

Do you charge more for wraps in suburbs vs Chicago?

No. Wrap pricing is the same across all of Chicagoland. Your zip code doesn't change what we charge.

How long does it take if you have to come pick up my truck?

Pickup adds zero time to the install. We schedule pickup the morning install starts. Total turnaround is the same: 5–9 business days from approval to delivery, regardless of whether your fleet is in Naperville or Lincoln Park.

Can you handle a multi-city fleet rollout?

Yes. We regularly rotate large fleets through the bay 2–3 vehicles at a time so the rest stay on the road. We've done this for HVAC, electrical, plumbing, and Amazon DSP fleets across Cook, DuPage, Lake, and Will counties.

Where is your shop?

4711 N Lamon Ave #7, Chicago, IL 60630 — Mayfair / Northwest Side, just east of I-94. About 25 minutes from the Loop, 35 from O'Hare, 45 from Naperville without traffic.
