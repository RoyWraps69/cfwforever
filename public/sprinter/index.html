Sprinter Wrap Design Considerations

Sprinter vans have unique body lines that require specialized templates:

  • Sliding door integration — vinyl is cut along the door track so it opens and closes without catching
  • Rear door panels — twin or single rear doors with separate panels for each
  • High-roof transitions — seamless design flow from lower body to the extended roof panel
  • Wheel well contours — precise template cuts around complex wheel arch shapes
  • Bumper and trim integration — optional coverage for chrome or painted bumpers

How much does a Sprinter van wrap cost?

A full Sprinter van wrap costs $3,750–$6,600 depending on wheelbase and roof height. Partial wraps start at $1,200. All pricing includes design, cast vinyl, UV overlaminate, and free pickup.

Do you wrap high-roof Sprinters?

Yes. High-roof and super high-roof Sprinters are our specialty. The additional surface area means more branding space and higher visibility on the road.

How long does a Sprinter wrap take?

Design: 2–5 business days. Installation: 2–3 days. Delivery back same day or next day.

Can you wrap a Sprinter with a sliding door?

Yes. Sliding door wraps are standard. The vinyl is precision-cut along the door track for clean operation.

Will a wrap damage my Sprinter's paint?

No. Cast vinyl wraps are fully removable without paint damage. Wraps actually protect the factory paint from UV, road salt, and minor scratches.
