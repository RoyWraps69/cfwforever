Sprinter vans have unique body lines that require specialized templates:

ROI & Results

Protecting Your Investment: Sprinter Van Wrap Care & Maintenance

Maintaining the pristine appearance and longevity of your Sprinter van wrap from Chicago Fleet Wraps is straightforward, ensuring your mobile advertisement continues to look sharp for years. Regular cleaning is crucial; we recommend hand washing your wrapped Sprinter using mild, non-abrasive detergents and a soft cloth or sponge. Avoid harsh chemicals, abrasive brushes, or high-pressure washers, especially near edges, as these can damage the vinyl film.

For optimal results, wash your wrapped Sprinter van at least once a week, or more frequently if it accumulates excessive dirt, grime, or pollutants, which are common on Chicago's busy roads. Promptly remove any bird droppings, tree sap, or fuel spills, as these substances can stain or degrade the vinyl over time if left untreated. A gentle spray of water followed by a soft wipe is usually sufficient for these minor incidents.

While automatic car washes can be tempting for convenience, we strongly advise against them for wrapped vehicles. The harsh brushes, aggressive detergents, and high-pressure water jets commonly used in these facilities can lift edges, scratch the vinyl, and significantly reduce the lifespan of your Sprinter wrap. Stick to hand washing to preserve the integrity and vibrant colors of your investment.

To further protect your wrap and enhance its sheen, consider applying a liquid wax or sealant specifically designed for vinyl wraps every few months. This adds an extra layer of protection against UV rays, environmental contaminants, and minor abrasions, helping to keep your Sprinter van wrap looking fresh and new, reflecting the professionalism of your Chicago-based business.
