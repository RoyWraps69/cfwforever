The complete car wrap process from initial consultation to finished vehicle typically takes 7-10 business days:

Frequently Asked Questions About Car Wraps in Chicago

How much does it cost to wrap a car in Chicago?

A full car wrap in Chicago costs $2,800-$5,500 depending on vehicle size and design complexity. Color change wraps range from $3,200-$6,000. Partial wraps start at $1,200. These prices include design, production, and professional installation with Avery Dennison or 3M cast vinyl.

How long does a car wrap last in Chicago?

A professional car wrap using cast vinyl lasts 5-7 years in Chicago's climate, including harsh winters with road salt and hot summers. Proper care — hand washing, avoiding automatic car washes with brushes, and parking in shade when possible — maximizes wrap lifespan.

Can I wrap a leased car?

Yes. Vinyl wraps are fully removable without paint damage, making them ideal for leased vehicles. When the lease ends, the wrap is removed and the vehicle returns to original condition. Many businesses wrap leased fleet vehicles for this exact reason.

Is a car wrap cheaper than painting?

Yes, significantly. A quality paint job costs $5,000-$15,000+ and is permanent. A vinyl wrap costs $2,800-$5,500, lasts 5-7 years, protects the original paint, and is fully reversible. For commercial vehicles, wraps are also 100% tax-deductible as advertising expenses under IRS Section 179.
