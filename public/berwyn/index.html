Berwyn Fleet Wrap FAQ

How far is your shop from Berwyn?

Our shop at 4711 N. Lamon Ave in Chicago is approximately 9 miles from downtown Berwyn — about 20 minutes via I-290 or Harlem Avenue. We offer free fleet pickup and delivery directly to your Berwyn location, so distance is never a factor.

Do you offer fleet discounts for Berwyn businesses?

Yes. We offer volume discounts starting at 3% for 2–4 vehicles, 7% for 5–9, 11% for 10–24, and 15% for 25+ vehicles. Many Berwyn home service companies start with one or two vans and scale their fleet branding as they see the ROI from increased call volume.

What materials do you use for Berwyn fleet wraps?

We exclusively use Avery Dennison MPI 1105 and 3M IJ180-CV3 cast vinyl — both rated for 5–7 years of outdoor use. These premium materials resist cracking from Chicago's freeze-thaw cycles, road salt exposure, and UV damage throughout Illinois winters and summers.

How long does a fleet wrap installation take?

Cargo vans: 1–2 business days. Sprinter vans: 2–3 days. Box trucks (16–22 ft): 2–3 days. Box trucks (24–26 ft): 3–4 days. We phase fleet orders to minimize vehicle downtime — you're never without your entire fleet.

Can I get a partial wrap instead of a full wrap?

Absolutely. Partial wraps and spot graphics start under $1,500 and are a budget-friendly way to brand your Berwyn fleet. Many companies start with partial wraps and upgrade to full wraps later as they see the impact on lead generation.

Are fleet wraps tax deductible?

Yes. Commercial vehicle wraps are 100% tax deductible under IRS Section 179 as a business advertising expense. This means your entire fleet wrap investment can be written off in the year of purchase. Consult your tax professional for details specific to your situation.

What industries do you wrap in Berwyn?

We wrap vehicles for HVAC, plumbing, electrical, general contracting, landscaping, food service, delivery, and home service companies throughout Berwyn and Cook County. Any business with a vehicle is a candidate for fleet branding.
