How long do commercial vehicle wraps last?

Commercial vehicle wraps using premium cast vinyl (Avery Dennison MPI 1105 or 3M IJ180-CV3) are rated for 5”“7 years of outdoor use. Cast vinyl is the industry standard for commercial fleet wraps. Cast vinyl conforms to curves and rivets without lifting, unlike cheaper calendered vinyl which shrinks and fails within 1”“2 years.
